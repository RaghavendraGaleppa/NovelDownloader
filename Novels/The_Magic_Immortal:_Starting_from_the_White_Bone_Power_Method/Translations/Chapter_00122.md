# Chapter 122: Can a Top-Grade Mystic Tortoise Be Contracted?

Ning Jingming summoned the bronze vat and hurled it toward the head of a mid-grade Mystic Tortoise that was spewing black watery radiance from its maw. Simultaneously, he formed hand seals with his other hand and summoned a massive golden staff of superior quality, bringing it crashing down upon the creature's skull.
