# Chapter 421: On the Brink of Peril

The defenders of Feiniao Fort witnessed two powerful warriors willingly charging to their deaths, moved by their sacrifice. Seeing the enemy's meticulously constructed towers—where the three bloodstained bells now emitted thinning crimson mists and their tolling grew faint—their morale surged. Seizing the moment, they launched a fierce counterattack.

The results proved remarkably effective. They successfully repelled the demonic alliance forces, buying precious time for reinforcements to arrive.
