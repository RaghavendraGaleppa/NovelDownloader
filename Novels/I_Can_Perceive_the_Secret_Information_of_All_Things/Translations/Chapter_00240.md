# Chapter 240: The Catch

From the moment he formulated his plan, Xiao Ji had a clear objective. Directly snatching the Dragon Fish would allow the Hunchbacked Angler to fully utilize the power of his Divine Wood Fishing Rod. Moreover, with the surrounding Weak Water Sect disciples—though merely at the Foundation Establishment stage—present near the Sunken Coffin Lake, their combined efforts could still pose minor complications for him. 

In that scenario, the best outcome would be successfully seizing the Dragon Fish, but the gains would be relatively modest. 

Alternatively, directly attacking the Hunchbacked Angler wasn't a bad idea either.
