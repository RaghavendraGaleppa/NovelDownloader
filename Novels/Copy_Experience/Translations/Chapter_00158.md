Chapter 156: Is the Island Taken?

Sometimes, one couldn't help but sigh at the vast differences between cultivators of different races and types.

Human cultivators, without exception, had to undergo years of secluded cultivation to advance to the Golden Core stage. Even the legendary Heavenly Spiritual Root holders, who faced no bottlenecks in forming their cores and were undoubtedly the fastest, still required at least five years. As for those with slightly inferior aptitude, insufficient accumulation, or less powerful cultivation methods, even if they were fortunate enough to form a core, it would take them thirty to forty years.
