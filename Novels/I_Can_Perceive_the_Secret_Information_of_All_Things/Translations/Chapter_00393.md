# Chapter 393: The Final Gourd  

The Earth King Sovereign held a purple gourd in one hand while pointing at the fire hound that had leaped from the volcano with the other. "It's been a long time since I've suppressed something this massive," he remarked.  

The fire hound appeared to be an enormous dog, but from a biological standpoint, it merely resembled one. Molten lava coursed through its body, flowing with every movement...
