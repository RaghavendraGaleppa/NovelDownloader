Facing the two of them, Ge Qing Shen asked again, "Have you two junior brothers considered it? Don't worry, senior brother will definitely compensate for the loss of immortal crystals. This was the first time he had truly come into contact with someone from the Celestial Realm lineage. He was truly shocked. Such a self-important personality, he wondered in what environment it had grown. It was not easy to survive until now.

However, Zhang Jing knew in his heart that there shouldn't be many such people in the Celestial Realm lineage. Otherwise, within the Taiyi Boundless Dao Sect, the Nine Regions lineage wouldn't have been suppressed so miserably by the Celestial Realm lineage. "Could this person be cannon fodder pushed out by the Celestial Realm lineage?" Zhang Jing frowned and guessed.

But then, as if he had figured something out, a relaxed smile appeared on his face. Was there a need to think so much? No matter who the opponent was, he would just crush them all.

At that moment, Ge Qing Shen stood there, staring blankly at the backs of the two figures moving further and further away, unable to recover for a long time. These two people... actually ignored his request. They didn't even give him, Ge Qing Shen, any face. He had already said that he could compensate them with immortal crystals, what else did they want? Ge Qing Shen was instantly furious. His blood surged, and his face turned visibly red.

"Heh, they don't understand any manners, and they don't even respect their senior brother! The Nine Regions lineage from the lower realm is indeed a bunch of mud legs, inferior humans with incomplete Dao principles in their bodies," he thought bitterly.

After taking a few deep breaths, Ge Qing Shen's face returned to normal. Then, he looked at the three people opposite him, cupped his hands, and said politely, "Ge is not strict in his discipline, and these disciples from the Nine Regions lineage are really difficult to teach, so the two incompetent junior brothers offended the three of you. I hope Fellow Daoist Shi and the others don't take it to heart."

"But don't worry, if we meet in the secret realm, Ge will definitely teach those two a good lesson."

"That, Brother Ge, those two people, you might..." The leading man among the three said with a strange expression. He really couldn't understand where Ge Qing Shen, whose strength was only slightly stronger than his, got the confidence to say he would teach those two Foundation Establishment monsters a lesson. And discipline... this guy really dared to say it. He had originally planned to remind the other party, but at that moment, the man suddenly changed his mind.
