The dark, ancient hammer hung silently, inverted, at the boundary between ice and fire. It was approximately three hundred paces from the cave entrance.

"Hoo..." Li Mo exhaled a plume of white mist, his forehead slick with sweat. After walking a hundred paces, he was already drenched, his very bones itching from the vibrations within. He was exhausted, but the light in Li Mo's eyes grew brighter. The divine weapon tempered his bones; with every step, he could feel his qi and blood strengthening, his physique being honed to greater power. In just a hundred paces, he had actually opened another meridian.

Thirteen meridians.

For normal martial artists, twelve meridians was the limit, at which point they would begin condensing blood into qi, transforming internal energy into breath. Li Mo had now completely surpassed the Qi and Blood Realm. Furthermore, his progress was far from over.

One hundred and forty paces.

"The pressure is getting more intense." Li Mo's steps became increasingly difficult, his physical body reaching its limit at this point. Fine lines of blood faintly appeared on his skin. He had already used the Extreme Soldier Body method as much as possible to reduce the damage caused by the weapon's sharp killing aura. But a divine weapon was still a divine weapon.

"Fortunately, I still have some martial dao insights left," he muttered. "Otherwise, I'd have to stop here today..." Li Mo still wanted to try.
