**Chapter 79: Guest Elder of the Merchant Alliance**

In his own courtyard, Lu Xu dismissed most of the Qi Condensation cultivators who had followed him, retaining only his two acquaintances, Master Wang Fu and Mo Qingchan.

"Senior," Master Wang Fu asked, unable to hold back the many questions he had suppressed in his heart, "May I presumptuously ask, did you achieve Foundation Establishment on your own?"

A smile appeared on Lu Xu's face. "My two fellow Daoists, there's no need to be so reserved," he said. "You guessed correctly. I did indeed achieve Foundation Establishment on my own!"
