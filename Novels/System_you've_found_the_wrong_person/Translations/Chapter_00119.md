# Chapter 119: Sleeping Under a Tree, a Blade Falls from the Sky  

Northern Jin, Changzhou.  

Dou Changsheng sat on the ground, leaning against a large tree. He held a waterskin high in his hand, gazing up at the crescent moon hanging in the night sky.  

"I'm only nineteen years old!"  

Dou Changsheng let out a long sigh, overwhelmed with emotion.  

Too much had happened in such a short time...
