# Chapter 87: Dou Changsheng Dies from Poison

Carrying a scroll on his back, Dou Changsheng walked step by step toward the third checkpoint, the Hero's Sword hanging at his waist. As he lifted his gaze to the towering fortress before him, he saw rows of armored soldiers lining the walls, their figures crowding the battlements. Countless eyes were fixed upon him in unison.

Dou Changsheng's gaze remained sharp and unwavering.
