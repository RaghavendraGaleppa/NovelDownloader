# Chapter 84: True Lord Zhongli

“Besides that third-tier spirit vein, there are also more than ten second-tier spirit veins in the Violet Lightning Sparrow’s territory. The Merchant Alliance won’t fully occupy all of them!” Yun Zhenzi said, his words filled with hidden meaning.

Lu Xu understood what he was implying. The Merchant Alliance was likely planning to offer these spirit veins as incentives to meritorious Foundation Establishment cultivators.

A single second-tier spirit vein, even the lowest-grade one, meant a Heavenly Spirit Flower every three hundred years, and it meant a Foundation Establishment immortal clan like the Mo family of Qinghu.
