# Chapter 117: The Four-Stringed Pipa Unites, A Song of Winter Snow Arrives  

Hongyun City.  

The citizens took to the streets, discussing the destruction that had ravaged the city, while many worked tirelessly through the night to repair the damage and rescue those in need.  

In a deserted pleasure house,  

moonlight filtered through the crimson clouds, casting an eerie glow.  

A man who looked every bit the seasoned warrior sat amidst the ruins. Before him stood an untouched table, upon which rested two cups of wine.  

"After all, you were still deceived, weren’t you? Such is the nature of human hearts," mused Sanji Dao, the Three-Season Blade. "How I’d love to find a few lesser demons to vent my frustrations on..."
