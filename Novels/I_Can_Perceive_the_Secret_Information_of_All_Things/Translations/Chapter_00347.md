# Chapter 347: Journey to the Endless Sand Sea  

Ye Zhaixing made his move—only a single sword strike.  

That was all he could manage. In the process of resurrecting Hongzhu, he had even sacrificed his spiritual roots, all for the sake of allowing his beloved wife to see the beauty of this world once more.  

Even if it was just for a single day, a single hour, a fleeting moment—just long enough to say a final farewell—it was worth it.
