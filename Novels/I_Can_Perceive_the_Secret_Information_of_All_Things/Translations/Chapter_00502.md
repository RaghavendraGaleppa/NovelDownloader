# Chapter 502: The Tragic Pasture

The Beast Lord of Ten Thousand Realms was an ancient immortal of the Great Luo realm, a being who had once dared to speak back to the Immortal Emperor. Xiao Ji naturally had no desire to confront him directly.

Through his perception, Xiao Ji gauged their respective combat strengths and concluded the odds were even. Had they been outside, he could have ended the battle swiftly. But within the opponent's grand grotto-heaven, the situation grew perilous.
