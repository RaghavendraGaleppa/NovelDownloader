# Chapter 126: The Nether Daoist's Century-Old Plan Goes Up in Smoke

Rituals and music had collapsed, the world had grown cold and unfeeling.

First, the Empress had bestowed upon him the title of "Loyal to the Throne," followed by one extraordinary achievement after another.

Yet he was still suspected of disloyalty to the Great Jin Dynasty?

Where was justice in this world?

The atmosphere grew heavy in an instant. What should have been an explosive confrontation never materialized, leaving the situation to take a bizarre turn.
