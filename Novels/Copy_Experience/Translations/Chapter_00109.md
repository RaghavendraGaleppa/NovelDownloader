## Chapter 108: Longevity Token

Carrying the corpse of the Black Robe Society leader, Lu Xu walked towards the massive clearing left by the explosion. A basin had formed here, with exposed, hard rock at its bottom.

Mo Qingchan sat leaning against a large tree at the edge of the basin, a faint trickle of blood spilling from the corner of her mouth.

Her face was excessively pale, her beautiful features revealing a hint of fragility, like a wounded fawn licking its wounds alone.

"How are you?" Lu Xu walked over and asked.
