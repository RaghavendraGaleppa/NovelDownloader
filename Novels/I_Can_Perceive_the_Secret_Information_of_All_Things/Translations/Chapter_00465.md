# Chapter 465: The Mouth of Nine Abysses, Nine Treasures United  

"Only when the Nine Treasures unite can their true power be unleashed."  

This truth was spoken by the Undying Sword Immortal Wan Jiayi, and a similar answer had been revealed within the remnants of the First Sage's shadow.  

Thus, Xiao Ji set off for the Mouth of the Nine Abysses.  

The last time the Nine Treasures were forged into one had been at the Mouth of the Nine Abysses.  

Now, Xiao Ji was heading there once more...
