# Chapter 103: Moon-Sun Pill

"Return to Wei Kingdom, return to Qinghu Market?"

Hearing this, Mo Qingchan's heart trembled. How could she not want to? This was something she yearned for, her greatest obsession besides achieving Foundation Establishment. Since the day Qinghu Market was breached by the Black Robe Society, her loving ancestor died, and she lost her home and kin, forced to wander without a stable place. Not a single day passed that she didn't dream of fighting her way back to Qinghu Market, to eradicate the Black Robe Society and kill the culprit who destroyed the Mo family...
