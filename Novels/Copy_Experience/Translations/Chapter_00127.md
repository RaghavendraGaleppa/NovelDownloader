Chapter 126: Upholding Justice

This sudden turn of events startled everyone, leaving them dumbfounded and unable to react for a long time.

The change came too quickly. One moment, the two Peak Foundation Establishment cultivators from Thousand Absolute Sect were jointly dealing with the Wei family cultivators, and the next, the two of them had turned hostile towards each other.

The Grand Elder of Thousand Absolute Sect was actually a member of the Wei family?

This was truly unexpected and incredibly hard to believe. All along, this person had been the most aggressive and unyielding towards the Wei family, yet now he had directly betrayed them.
