# Chapter 385: Extra - Ying Xiao's Diary, The Beginning  

Ying Xiao found herself missing home.  

Yet she wasn't sure if it could still be called *her* home.  

Standing by the ancient road outside the pavilion, she gazed toward the direction of Dragon's Peak City, now obscured by layers of towering mountains.  

"I *did* run away from home," she muttered to herself. "There's no way I'm going back. I have to make Father Emperor realize that his little warm jacket has sprung a leak."
