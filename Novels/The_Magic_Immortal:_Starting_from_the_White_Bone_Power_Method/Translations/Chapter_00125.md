# Chapter 125: Fatherly Love and Filial Piety

"Ribbit?"

The silver toad's remaining large eye showed a human-like expression of confusion as it stared intently at Wang Chan.

Under its prolonged gaze, Wang Chan began to feel somewhat embarrassed.

Only then did the silver toad seem to understand Wang Chan's meaning. It let out a soft croak and then shook its head.

Seeing the silver toad's refusal, Wang Chan could only sigh in resignation.
