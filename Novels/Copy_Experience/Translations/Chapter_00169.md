"Don't be afraid, we just want you to tell the truth!"

When these words rang in Lu Xu's ears, they immediately stirred an even stronger sense of resistance within him. At the same time, a hint of fear also arose in his heart; he hadn't expected such a test behind this bronze door.

Telling the truth was absolutely impossible. Lu Xu harbored many secrets, and he couldn't allow himself to be forced into a situation where he had to reveal anything.
