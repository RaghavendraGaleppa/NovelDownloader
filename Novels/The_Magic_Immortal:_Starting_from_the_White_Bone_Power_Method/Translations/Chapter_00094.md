# Chapter 94: Brother Wang, Help Me!

Unconsciously, the time had reached the beginning of the mao hour (5-7 am).  

Wang Chan carefully tidied up the basement before returning to the talisman-making chamber to begin crafting talismans.  

As usual, he attempted three low-grade talismans and succeeded with two.  

After three hours of talisman crafting, Wang Chan quickly packed up and headed out to practice medicine.
