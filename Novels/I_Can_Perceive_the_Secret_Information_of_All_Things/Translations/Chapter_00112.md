# Chapter 112: The Undercover Agent and His Handler

The Death's Gate branch leader was writing a letter to the Dao Alliance, addressed to his handler codenamed Stone Lotus.

Yet Stone Lotus seemed to have gone silent—no matter how many letters he sent, there was no response.

"The original plan was set for the Winter Solstice, but circumstances have changed. We may need to move earlier... I can't delay any longer. If I wait any more, the Death's Gate sect master will surely grow suspicious of me. Send a master cultivator quickly. I'll try to hold out until the Winter Solstice. Under no circumstances should I be the one to obtain the four-stringed pipa fragment from Young Master Wuyou—that..."
