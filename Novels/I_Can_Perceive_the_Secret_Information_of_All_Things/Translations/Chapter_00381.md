# Chapter 381: Soaring Tiger in the Heavens

The Heavenly Tiger decided to rest for a while—its mind was currently in a daze.

It simply couldn't stand dragons being more arrogant than itself.

But these were genuine dragons, over a hundred of them.  

The sudden tiger's roar had clearly startled the dragons. That single mighty cry was enough to shake them—several dozen even trembled in response. The true dragons scattered in confusion...
