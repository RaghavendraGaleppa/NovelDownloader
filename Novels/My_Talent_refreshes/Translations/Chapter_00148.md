# Chapter 148: Toying with a Qi Condensation Perfected Cultivator, Refusing Fairy Cen

Jiang Hongguang and his wife heard Chu Ning say this and once again began to lead the way. Then, the three of them flew on the wind while chatting.

After only a short while of travel, Chu Ning directly stopped, then looked at Jiang Hongguang, saying with a wry smile: "Fellow Daoist Jiang, what do you mean by this? Rainbow Island was originally yours, and you invited me here simply as a helper. How did it end up with me having to be the island master?" Saying this, Chu Ning shook his head. Understanding their inner apprehensions, he now said to both of them with a sincere expression: "Fellow Daoist Jiang, I am used to being a carefree wanderer and dedicated to arduous cultivation. I truly have no interest whatsoever in being an island master. The island should remain as it was; I just need to find a place for a cave dwelling. As for that Tier 2 spiritual vein..." Saying this, Chu Ning paused, and Jiang Hongguang and his wife's gazes both fell upon Chu Ning. Although the two had already agreed that if Chu Ning forcibly took the Tier 2 spiritual vein, they would accept it, before the matter was finalized, they undoubtedly still held a glimmer of hope.

Chu Ning saw their concerned gazes and smiled, saying: "I originally wanted to wait until it was confirmed before telling you, but it's fine if we go together now. I suspect there might be more than just the hidden spiritual vein you discovered on this island."

"What?" Upon hearing Chu Ning's words, Jiang Hongguang and his wife instantly looked surprised.

Chu Ning then reached into his spirit beast pouch and patted it, releasing Little White. The little fellow immediately jumped onto Chu Ning's shoulder. At this moment, Chu Ning continued: "This spirit beast I raised is quite sensitive to spiritual energy. It discovered another place on this island where the spiritual energy is dense. As for whether it's a hidden spiritual vein, we'll need to investigate it further." As he spoke, Chu Ning then said to the two: "Why don't we go take a look now?"

"Oh, okay, okay." Jiang Hongguang, who had been somewhat stunned, came back to his senses, and then said: "Everything as Fellow Daoist Chu arranges."

Chu Ning didn't say much more at this moment. He immediately patted Little White lightly. The little fellow understood and began to direct the way. The three then continued to fly forward. First, they arrived at the desolate mountain where Chu Ning and Jiang Hongguang's group had been last time. Then, Little White directed them to continue flying forward. They flew all the way to the southwestern direction of the island, almost directly leaving the island. Only then did Little White signal Chu Ning to stop.

"Swallow Peak? Fellow Daoist Chu, are you talking about this place?" Jiang Hongguang looked at the mountain peak, which didn't have particularly abundant spiritual energy, and asked, somewhat bewildered.

"Whether it is or not, we'll only know by digging it open." As he spoke, Chu Ning roughly pinpointed a location, then directly patted another spirit beast pouch. Immediately, an Iron Rock Beast, about the size of a small calf, was released. In the past few months, although the Iron Rock Beast spent most of its time in the spirit beast pouch, Chu Ning still fed it daily. Especially during this past month after entering the Thousand Lakes and Islands, Chu Ning frequently released it to feed it in the stone room. This fellow grew much faster than Little White. After several months of growth, the Iron Rock Beast was already as big as a small calf. Jiang Hongguang and his wife exchanged glances, both surprised by the endless array of things Chu Ning possessed.

"Go, make a tunnel!"

Chu Ning lightly patted the Iron Rock Beast, and it immediately began burrowing into the side of the hill. Then, with Chu Ning's spells assisting in clearing the earth, a passage leading directly into the mountain's belly was quickly excavated. Chu Ning led the way, and the three entered in single file.
