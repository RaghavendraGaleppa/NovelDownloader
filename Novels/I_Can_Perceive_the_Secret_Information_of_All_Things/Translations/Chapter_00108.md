# Chapter 108: The Complex Situation in Redcloud City  

Redcloud City lived up to its name—the clouds above were dyed crimson, casting a somber hue over the entire settlement, as if the sky itself pressed heavily upon the rooftops.  

The city itself was small and worn, its streets and buildings bearing the marks of neglect, a clear sign that the local economy was far from thriving.  

Xiao Ji had agreed to let Three Seasons Blade accompany him for one primary reason: he didn’t want to appear alone in an unfamiliar place.  

Eating alone, staying alone at an inn, walking alone through strange streets—such solitude would only invite unwanted attention.
