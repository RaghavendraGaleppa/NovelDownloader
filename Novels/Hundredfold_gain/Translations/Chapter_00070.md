# Chapter 70: Celebrating Together, Qi Chan'er: Why Is Qi Chuan On Stage?

After purchasing the top-grade first-tier magic ink and talisman paper from the Treasure Pavilion, Qi Chuan secluded himself in his first-tier upper-grade cave dwelling, dedicating himself to crafting top-grade first-tier Five Elements Talismans.

During his remaining time, he maintained his daily routine of practicing the Evergreen Art to refine his spiritual energy. Although this wouldn't allow his cultivation to break through to the eighth level of Qi Refinement, it still helped steadily accumulate his foundation.
