# Chapter 248: Strategic Withdrawal  

Yun City.  

Inside a lavish mansion.  

Dou Changsheng and the Ninth Master sat respectfully in the lower seats, while a woman dressed in purple robes occupied the position of honor at the head of the room.  

As for the actual owner of this grand estate, he wasn’t even permitted to enter. Like a mere servant, he waited outside the door, attending to their needs. Though he was one of Yun City’s most prominent figures, his status here was no different from that of a menial laborer.
