# Chapter 451: The Ten-Year Deadline

泥老头目光大盛，又是敬畏，又是狂热。
泥老头目光灼灼地望着真武峰顶，若他有机会铸就道基的话，他自然也想成就那神武位格。
他浑浊的眼中闪过一丝精光，仿佛已经看到自己身披玄武星纹道袍的模样。
要是再敢想一下，这老迈的身躯若能借神武道基重焕生机，说不定还能在寿元将尽前搏一把金丹大道。

The Old Mud’s eyes shone with awe and fervor.
He stared intently at the summit of Zhenwu Peak. If he ever had the chance to forge a Dao Foundation, he too would naturally wish to achieve that Divine Martial status.
A sharp light flashed through his turbid eyes, as if he could already see himself wearing a Dao robe patterned with Black Tortoise star markings.
If he dared to dream a little further—if this aging body could borrow the Divine Martial Dao Foundation to regain its vitality—he might even be able to fight for the Golden Core Great Dao before his lifespan ran out.

最震撼的当属麻衣老道，他本在崖边修习《坐忘经》，此刻却被迫从“坐忘无我”之境惊醒。
老道本是无悲无喜的面孔首次冒出一缕惊容。
毕竟，他还是头一次见得如此狂猛的天地之灵气被以鲸吞之势牵引。
众人目光隔空交汇，皆看到彼此眼中的骇然。
筑基真人牵动天地之力本不足为奇，但能同时引动星力、剑意、戊土、玄灵等截然不同的道韵，简直闻所未闻！

The most shocked was the Old Daoist in hemp clothes. He had been practicing the *Sitting in Forgetfulness Sutra* by the cliff edge but was forcibly awakened from the state of "Sitting in Forgetfulness, No Self."
For the first time, a look of shock appeared on the old daoist's originally emotionless face.
After all, it was the first time he had witnessed such a violent torrent of natural spiritual energy being drawn in like a whale swallowing water.
The gazes of the crowd met through the air, seeing the horror in each other's eyes.
It was not strange for a Foundation Establishment True Person to manipulate the power of heaven and earth, but to simultaneously summon star power, sword intent, Earth element, and Xuanling—completely different Dao rhymes—was simply unheard of!

玄都八景，炼诸般玄妙，自然引得瞩目。
所幸真武峰周围百里皆是其主宰范围，又有徐云帆亲自布下，接连不断升级的隐星蔽月阵。
五年后的立春日，真武峰顶骤然响起一声清越长啸。
徐云帆周身真武法力激荡，声浪如龙吟九霄，震得方圆十里的云海翻腾倒卷。
那啸声中蕴含着真武伏魔的降魔道韵、万乘剑意的锋锐之气、青龙雷珠的霹雳之威，更有八景云气的玄妙变化。
九重云霭在这多重道韵冲击下，竟如薄纱般被层层撕裂，露出湛蓝天穹。
真武峰新进的弟子们骇然抬头，只见一道身影踏云直上，衣袂翻飞间，四方光华在周身流转交织。

The Eight Sceneries of Xuandu, refining all kinds of mysteries, naturally attracted attention.
Fortunately, within a hundred miles of Zhenwu Peak was his domain, and Xu Yunfan had personally set up the Star Concealing and Moon Hiding Array, which he had continuously upgraded.
On the day of Lichun five years later, a clear and resonant long howl suddenly rang out from the summit of Zhenwu Peak.
Xu Yunfan's Zhenwu magical power surged around him, the sound waves like a dragon roaring in the ninth heaven, shaking the sea of clouds within ten miles into a turbulent reversal.
The howl contained the Dao rhyme of Zhenwu subduing demons, the sharp edge of the Ten Thousand Chariots Sword Intent, the thunderous might of the Azure Dragon Thunder Pearl, and the subtle changes of the Eight Sceneries Cloud Qi.
Under the impact of these multiple Dao rhymes, the nine layers of clouds were actually torn apart layer by layer like thin gauze, revealing the azure sky.
The new disciples of Zhenwu Peak looked up in shock, seeing a figure stepping on clouds and rising straight up, with clothes fluttering, and the brilliance of the four directions flowing and interweaving around him.

有些嚣张过头了。
徐云帆迅速收敛气机，瞥了眼自己布下的隐星蔽月阵依旧运转无碍，顿时放下心来。
‘你的玄都八景炼天造化云笈入门了’
看着视界上属性面板提示栏上闪过的一条信息，徐云帆忍不住露出一抹笑意。
这玄都八景的修行之路，远比想象中更为艰难。徐云帆耗费整整五年光阴，才勉强将八景云气一一凝练成形。
期间不知经历多少次云气相冲的爆裂，多少次灵力反噬的痛楚，那些在静室中反复推演云气组合的日夜，那些因细微操控失误而功亏一篑的挫败，实在不足为外人道也。
每一次尝试将坎水云与离火云相融，都需要他将心神分割成数百缕，如同绣娘穿针引线般精准调控每一处灵力节点。
稍有不慎，便是云气爆裂、洞府狼藉的下场。
五年间，他记不清更换过多少套道袍，修补过多少次被炸毁的炼器台。
最艰难时，他曾在临字态下连续推演七日七夜，将八景云气的八万六千种变化组合一一尝试。
待退出临字态时，七窍都渗出了血丝。但正是这般近乎偏执的苦修，才让他在无数次失败后，终于抓住了那一丝玄妙的平衡之道。
如此终得了入门。
胸中郁气一朝泄，自然让他忍不住仰天长啸。
他现在也有些明白为何网文中总是有人一出关就忍不住仰天长啸。
被憋闷这么久，一朝得悟，终于天晴气朗，自然忍不住想要大吼几声泄泄郁气。

It was a bit too arrogant.
Xu Yunfan quickly retracted his aura. A glance at the Star Concealing and Moon Hiding Array he had set up confirmed it was still operating without issue, and he immediately put his mind at ease.
*[You have reached the Entry Level of the Xuandu Eight Sceneries Refining Heaven Creation Cloud Manual.]*
Looking at the message flashing on the attribute panel in his vision, Xu Yunfan couldn't help but smile.
The path of cultivating the Eight Sceneries of Xuandu was far more difficult than he had imagined. Xu Yunfan spent a full five years just to barely condense the Eight Sceneries Cloud Qi into form.
During that time, he experienced unknown numbers of explosions from clashing clouds and the pain of spiritual energy backlash. The days and nights spent repeatedly deducing cloud combinations in seclusion, the frustration of failure due to slight control errors—these were truly unspeakable.
Every time he attempted to fuse the Kan Water Cloud with the Li Fire Cloud, he had to split his mind into hundreds of threads, precisely regulating every node of spiritual energy like an embroiderer threading a needle.
If he was even slightly careless, the result was a cloud explosion and a ruined cave dwelling.
Over the five years, he lost count of how many sets of Dao robes he had changed and how many times he had repaired the exploded alchemy platform.
At the most difficult times, he stayed in the 'Lin' state for seven days and seven nights, trying out the eighty-six thousand combinations of the Eight Sceneries Cloud Qi one by one.
By the time he exited the 'Lin' state, blood was seeping from his seven orifices. But it was this almost paranoid bitter cultivation that allowed him, after countless failures, to finally grasp that subtle way of balance.
Thus, he finally reached the entry level.
The depression in his chest was released in one go, naturally making him unable to resist howling at the sky.
He now somewhat understood why in web novels, people always couldn't help but howl at the sky as soon as they came out of seclusion.
After being suppressed for so long, finally achieving enlightenment and seeing the sky clear and the air fresh, he naturally couldn't help but want to shout a few times to vent his gloom.

时间如流水，真正质变发生在第十年深冬。
当徐云帆将神武道基的三首六臂法相与《真武伏魔真经》所载的龟蛇真形相迭合时，整座真武峰的灵气骤然为之一滞。
只见峰顶云海翻涌间，一尊前所未有的真武法相拔地而起。
其本相乃玄天上帝之姿，身披北斗七星纹玄甲，脚踏龟蛇盘绕的灵台，脑后悬浮着真武北斗轮盘，七颗星辰熠熠生辉。
而这尊法相更融合了三首六臂的凶煞之相，主首怒目圆睁，额间天眼迸射降魔金光。
左首闭目凝神，眉心浮现临字道纹，右首则呈悟道之态，唇齿开合间似在诵念《真武伏魔》真言。
六臂各持神兵，前双臂作真武伏魔印，掌心龟蛇虚影吞吐星力。
中左臂持玄铁重剑，剑身缠绕青龙雷纹，中右臂抡动撼岳锤，锤头北斗七宿流转，后左臂倒提方天画戟，戟尖雷火交织。
最惊人的是法相背后隐隐浮现的洞天虚影。
当法相完全显化的刹那，整座真武峰的灵气如百川归海，竟在法相周围形成肉眼可见的灵气真空！

Time flowed like water, and the true qualitative change occurred in the deep winter of the tenth year.
When Xu Yunfan overlapped the Three-Headed Six-Armed Form of the Divine Martial Dao Foundation with the Turtle and Snake True Form recorded in the *Zhenwu Subduing Demon Sutra*, the spiritual energy of the entire Zhenwu Peak suddenly stagnated.
Amidst the roiling sea of clouds at the summit, an unprecedented Zhenwu Dharma Form rose from the ground.
Its main form was the posture of the Profound Heavenly God, wearing dark armor patterned with the Seven Stars of the Big Dipper, standing on a spiritual platform coiled by a turtle and snake, with the Zhenwu Big Dipper Wheel suspended behind his head, seven stars shining brightly.
This Dharma Form further fused the fierce appearance of three heads and six arms. The main head glared with wide eyes, and a Heavenly Eye between its brows shot out golden light that subdued demons.
