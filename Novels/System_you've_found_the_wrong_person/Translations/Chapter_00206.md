# Chapter 204: Lord Yama, the Festival of the Dead  

The State of Lu was a peculiar kingdom.  

Surrounded by Eastern Qi to its north and east, and Southern Chen to its south and west, it stood as a buffer between the two great powers. Five hundred years ago, several smaller states had dotted the lands around Lu, but over time, they were all swallowed by larger nations. Now, Lu had no room left for expansion.
