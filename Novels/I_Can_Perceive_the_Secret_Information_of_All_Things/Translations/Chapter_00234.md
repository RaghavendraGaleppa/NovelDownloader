# Chapter 234: Buying a Mountain

The journey to the Valley of Tremors had left Xiao Ji with both enmities and grudges. He had offended the Weak Water Sect—a mysterious and terrifying organization—and now knew that one of their elders was personally coming for him.

As for when, where, or how the elder would strike, Xiao Ji remained uncertain for now.  

That day, he had sensed the hunchbacked elder's intentions, but by the time he reacted and turned back to confront him, the man had already vanished.  

The incident weighed on Xiao Ji's mind. If only he could have eliminated the elder directly...
