# Chapter 111: Foundation Establishment

"Boom!"

A tremendous roar erupted from Zhang Jingxiu's body as blood seeped from all his pores. The intense surge of blood qi caused the surrounding temperature to rise sharply.

Zhou Qing swiftly moved to Zhang Jingxiu's side, producing golden needles. In the blink of an eye, the needles were inserted into all his vital acupoints.

The bleeding from his pores ceased, and the internal roaring gradually subsided.

"I failed," Zhang Jingxiu sighed heavily.

Zhou Qing frowned.
