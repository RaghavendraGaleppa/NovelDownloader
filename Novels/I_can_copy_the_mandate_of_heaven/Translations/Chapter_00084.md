Chapter 83: Unchanged Local Accent

Li Zhiming stopped on the hillside, gazing into the distance at the departing figure of Han Li for a long time. As Han Li rode his horse away, he almost completely disappeared from Li Zhiming's sight, never once looking back. Finally, Li Zhiming couldn't help but shake his head in dismay and turn to leave.

On the road, Han Li rode his horse at a gallop. The sky was gradually turning pale, with the first hints of dawn breaking on the horizon.
