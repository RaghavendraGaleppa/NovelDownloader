# Chapter 101: My Poor Brother and Sister

Ranked fifteenth on the Human Ranking.

The latest release of the Human Ranking caused an uproar. Dou Changsheng had initially ranked seventy-ninth, then soared to fifteenth in the second listing. His meteoric rise led many to speculate that in the next edition of the rankings, he would break into the top ten. These predictions were stated with absolute certainty, even more confidently than Dou Changsheng himself would have.
