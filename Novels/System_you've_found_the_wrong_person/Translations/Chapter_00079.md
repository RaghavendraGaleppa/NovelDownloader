# Chapter 79: My Brother is a Celestial  

Brothers—especially those who can share fortunes—are truly closer than blood brothers born of the same mother.  

As these words echoed in his mind, Dou Changsheng's gaze toward the old village chief shifted. Though the elder was hunched, balding, and weathered by time, none of it could conceal his innate handsomeness. He was, without a doubt, a dashing old man.
