# Chapter 201: Enlightenment, I Have Attained Enlightenment  

Time passed minute by minute.  

The world fell into complete silence. At some unknown moment, the rhythmic knocking sounds had ceased.  

The Second Elder sat with his eyelids drooping, motionless like a statue, his eyes closed in deep contemplation. Yet beneath his sleeves, his fingers trembled incessantly.  

His fingernails had dug deep into his flesh, drawing blood...
