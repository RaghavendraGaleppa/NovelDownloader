# Chapter 355: Jin'er  

Willow Town was enveloped in a misty rain.  

People walked the streets holding oil-paper umbrellas—through the lanes, along the paths outside the city walls...  

The townsfolk couldn't quite recall when exactly their home had changed, when it had become so safe. The tales told by the elders—of evil spirits snatching people from the mountains, of wicked cultivators swindling money and lives—now seemed like distant memories.
