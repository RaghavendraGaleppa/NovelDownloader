# Chapter 133: The Highest and Most Correct Expression of "Which Came First, the Chicken or the Egg?"

Standing atop a flat peak behind the Qingcang Sect, Xiao Ji, Qian Duoyu, and Zhao Jiuzhou found themselves surrounded by rows upon rows of chicken coops. They stood dumbfounded before an enormous cage, their minds nearly short-circuiting.

Inside the oversized coop, a massive hen with nine wings clucked furiously at them, even laying an egg in its agitation.

"So you're the legendary Nine-Winged Roc!" Xiao Ji murmured under his breath.

"Do you think Sect Leader Qin might be playing a joke on us?" Qian Duoyu wondered aloud.
