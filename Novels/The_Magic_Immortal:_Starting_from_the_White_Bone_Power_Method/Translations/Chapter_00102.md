# Chapter 102: Crimson Blood Contract Scroll

Bai Shan City.

Treasure Pavilion.

This upscale establishment in the eastern district appeared resplendent and golden from the outside, while its interior concealed opulence beneath an air of refined elegance.

Rumored to be backed by the Chu family—ranked third among the great clans of Mistcloud Kingdom—the Treasure Pavilion benefited from the Chus' profound legacy in artifact forging. Their reputation extended not only throughout Mistcloud Kingdom but also echoed across several neighboring nations.
