# Chapter 350: Beautiful Memories

Xiao Ji's sword slashed horizontally and vertically, carving countless intersecting lines in the air. These lines formed innumerable crosshatch patterns, resembling a vast spiritual energy net that descended toward the skeletal shadow beast. As it fell, the lines ignited with crimson flames.

The intricate web of fiery lines passed through the colossal beast's body like razor-thin wires slicing through soft tofu.
