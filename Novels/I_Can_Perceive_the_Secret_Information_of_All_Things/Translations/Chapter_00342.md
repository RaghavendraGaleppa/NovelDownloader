# Chapter 342: Red Candles, Red Veil

In the eerie bridal chamber of the underground space, Xiao Ji encountered a woman dressed in vibrant red wedding attire, her face covered by a crimson bridal veil.

Yet this was not the proprietress of the White Bone Inn.

The woman lay motionless upon a bed shrouded in spiritual energy and cold mist, her face concealed beneath the scarlet cloth.
