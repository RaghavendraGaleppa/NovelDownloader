At the heart of the Divine Jade, there was no sun or moon, only the radiant glow of the Jade Phoenix God.

If any demon race were here, just by sensing the aura within, their bloodline would become purer. This was the seed of hope the woman had left for the myriad races of future generations. As long as the Jade Phoenix God existed, the clans that had vanished were not truly extinct.

It was the hope left by the Nine-Colored Primordial Phoenix for all races.

Ying Bing lowered her head slightly, her jade-like face reflecting the magnificent light. Her eyelashes trembled slightly as she avoided looking at the Jade Phoenix God.

Then.

"Me?"

To be honest.

Other than having considerable wealth, a hammer talent unparalleled in the world, culinary skills that master various cuisines, and being somewhat handsome... what else was he?

The Jade Phoenix God wasn't just a divine artifact; it carried the weight of destiny. He felt he couldn't bear it.

Ice Cube was different. She had a Red Destiny, was the Phoenix Empress, and was so dazzlingly talented she could stand shoulder-to-shoulder with heaven-defying monsters.

She remembered that day under the Marriage Tree, when they were chatting casually. Li Mo was preoccupied with sending a letter to Fu Yingzi, worried about not finding the girl's father.

He talked about his journey's sights and experiences: the corrupt officials who inverted right and wrong, the border guards skilled at changing faces, the mother and daughter relying on each other, the food eaten by ordinary people in the southern frontier.

The Rift Sea Dragon Whale.

Then it charged into the Stone Monkey Divine Mark, becoming one of the Seventy-Two Transformations.

"It seems the Seventy-Tee Transformations will be perfected soon."

Li Mo felt a sense of swelling in his will and soul, a satisfied smile in his heart.

He decided to take a break and also try out this newly acquired Rift Sea Dragon Whale Transformation.
