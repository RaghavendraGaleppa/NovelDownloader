# Chapter 228: Finding the Demon's Memories

The dozen or so fishermen from the Ruoshui Sect searched the Valley of Tremors for many days but found nothing. Their patience had worn thin.

The hunchbacked elder didn't believe the thieves who had stolen their harvest had already left the area. However, with the inexplicable increase in second and third-tier demons in the region, it was no longer safe to stay. He ultimately led his people away.

Their departure didn't mean they were giving up the pursuit. The moment the thieves had appeared, the elder had managed to capture a trace of spiritual energy. As long as they guarded the entrance to the Valley...
