# Chapter 149: Sweeping Three Mountains, Pacifying Nine Strongholds (Part 2)  

The next day, just before dawn.  

Outside the desolate inn, County Lieutenant Zhou stood with a hundred soldiers, chewing on dry rations. When he saw Dou Changsheng emerge, he immediately set aside his food and hurried forward, bowing gratefully.  

"Thank you, hero, for saving our lives."  

Dou Changsheng waved a hand dismissively. "It was nothing..."
