# Chapter 519: Fishing for a Big Fish

The final circle should have been extremely tense, but Xiao Ji turned it into his personal stage. The lone wolves hiding in the shadows were mercilessly swept away by the devastating power of his giant axe, crushed like insects beneath a rolling wheel.

When the three immortals saw the Taoist priest beginning to lose his composure, they quickly fled far away, taking shelter at the edge of the thunder zone.
