# Chapter 114: The White Bone Annihilation Sword  

"Five hundred thousand!"  

The voice came from a man in Auction Room Eleven.  

The seven or eight bidders who had been competing immediately fell silent.  

A standard top-grade magical artifact with a single layer of restrictions was typically priced around two hundred thousand spirit coins on the market. However, due to their extreme rarity, they usually ended up selling for closer to three hundred thousand.
