# Chapter 312: Stunned by the Taoist's Wealth

The Taoist descended like a celestial being descending to the mortal realm, stepping upon clouds formed by coalesced spiritual energy. 

As the Taoist appeared, numerous cultivators began descending from the sky in succession. Like ravenous demons, they gazed greedily at the spiritual plants covering the ground. Their ruthless hands reached out without restraint, stuffing all manner of flowers, herbs, and trees into their storage artifacts.
