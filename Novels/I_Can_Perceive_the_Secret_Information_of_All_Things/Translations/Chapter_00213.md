# Chapter 213: The Power of Immortal Flames

A Golden Core cultivator daring to provoke a Nascent Soul expert—such an act could only mean he had a death wish.

The Northern Garrison General of River Valley Kingdom would never let such an individual go. Killing a Golden Core cultivator wouldn't require much effort, just a trivial expenditure of time.

He had already spent too many days in this sea of flames without catching even a glimpse of the legendary immortal fire. Frustrated, slaying an insignificant Golden Core cultivator would serve as a perfect way to vent his irritation.
