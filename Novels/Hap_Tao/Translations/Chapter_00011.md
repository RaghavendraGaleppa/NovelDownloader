# Chapter 11: Steady as a Dog

"Come on, take a hit from me if you dare, you coward! You spineless weakling!" After launching a flurry of attacks only to have Qin Ziling dodge every critical strike as if he could predict the future, Nangong Yue finally began losing his composure. He resorted to taunts and jeers, attempting to rattle Qin Ziling with words.

What Nangong Yue didn't know was that Qin Ziling had been a man of unshakable will in his previous life. Even when struck by ALS, he hadn't been broken—instead, his fighting spirit had only grown stronger. After his soul transmigrated and merged with the residual consciousness of an ancient demon, he gained even deeper insight into human cunning and worldly deceit. Nangong Yue's petty tricks were far from enough to unsettle him.

Qin Ziling remained calm, continuously shifting and dodging.

Steady as a dog!

Before long, Nangong Yue began showing signs of fatigue. His transitions between moves became less fluid, his speed slowed, and his openings grew more frequent and obvious.

Yet Qin Ziling didn't rush to strike. Beneath his composed expression, his eyes glinted with a chilling, serpentine coldness—like a viper lying in wait, ready to deliver a fatal blow at the perfect moment.

By now, everyone in the training grounds had been drawn to the duel between Nangong Yue and Qin Ziling. 

To them, Nangong Yue was clearly dominating. Qin Ziling could only evade, seemingly incapable of counterattacking, and it seemed only a matter of time before he slipped up and lost. Still, the fact that he had lasted this long was already astonishing to most.

Only Zuo Le watched with growing amazement. 

As a martial artist who had reached the Jinli (劲力) stage, he could see what others couldn't—Qin Ziling had been evading with perfect composure from start to finish, while Nangong Yue's breathing had grown ragged, his movements increasingly disjointed.

"Stop!"
