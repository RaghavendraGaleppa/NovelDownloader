# Chapter 111: Mid Foundation Establishment

A deeper pool of spiritual power appeared in his dantian. Above it, like falling sweet dew, drops of liquid spiritual power continuously descended.

"Drip! Drop!"

This was the most beautiful sound in the world. After eighteen consecutive drips, Lu Xu's dantian now contained eighteen drops of pure, clear azure spiritual power.

An incredibly powerful aura emanated from his body. Lu Xu opened his eyes, and two beams of azure light shot out from them.
