# Chapter 358: Xiao Ji's Breakthrough – What Is 'Emptiness'?

Jin'er sat atop the tallest building in the abandoned Ancient City of Tai, gently stroking her exquisite longsword as though caressing her very soul.  

To a sword cultivator, their blade was both their shadow and half their life.  

After admiring her treasured sword for a while, she hugged her knees and gazed at the distant sky. Her thoughts drifted to the shattered fragments of the Black Iron Seal—her master's inkstick. A pity it had already dissolved...
