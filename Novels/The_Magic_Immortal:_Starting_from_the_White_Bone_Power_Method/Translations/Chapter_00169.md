# Chapter 169: Ghost Monkey

The sky stretched clear and cloudless.  

In the distance, verdant mountains rose in layers while a mighty river surged endlessly. Before him lay a lush, thriving forest with towering ancient trees reaching toward the heavens.  

Wang Chan stood at the forest's edge, the dry yellow leaves beneath his feet soft and yielding. A breeze rushed toward him from within the woods, carrying with it the damp, earthy scent of the air.
