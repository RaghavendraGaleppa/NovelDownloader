# Chapter 173: Battle in the Sea of Consciousness

"I've been waiting for you!"

The moment True Immortal Muyuan entered, Lu Xu sensed him. His consciousness converged on the towering Yin God within his Sea of Consciousness. He raised his eyes and looked forward. Ripples spread through this Sea of Consciousness space, stirring up waves, as if a storm had erupted at sea. A colossal phantom forcibly squeezed in from above the firmament. Its features were indistinct, and its entire body shimmered with green light.
