# Chapter 415: The Saint's Remnant Shadow

Books in the third level of the Star Sea Library were not to be touched casually—they were like buried landmines, where a single misstep could lead to irreversible danger.  

Yet, Xiao Ji paid no heed to such perils.  

In this world, if there existed something—or someone—that Xiao Ji could not perceive, it would unsettle him deeply. After all, he possessed the ability to sense the hidden truths of all things.
