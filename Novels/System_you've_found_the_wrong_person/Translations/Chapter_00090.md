# Chapter 90: The Shock of Eastern Qi (Making Up for Yesterday!)

The fall of the third pass swept through Linzi like a raging storm, instantly sending tremors through the capital and striking fear into the imperial court.

From its very inception, the Seven Ultimate Passes had been established to guard against invasions from Northern Jin. Yet now, without the Northern Jin army even mobilizing, Dou Changsheng alone—armed with nothing but his sword—had seized two of the passes in succession.
