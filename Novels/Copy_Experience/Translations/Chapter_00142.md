Chapter 141: The Wei Family's Threat

Decades had passed, and everyone had made progress to varying degrees.

Although the cultivation base displayed by the other party was that of a Mid-Foundation Establishment cultivator, and he even looked like he had just entered the Mid-Foundation Establishment stage not long ago, Wei Yuanming always felt that the other party was probably not that simple. Unfathomable! Every time Wei Yuanming met Lu Xu, he would have this feeling, and this time...
