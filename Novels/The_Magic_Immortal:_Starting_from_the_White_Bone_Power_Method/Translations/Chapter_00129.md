# Chapter 129: Black Tiger, Are You Ready?

Wang Chan stood motionless, his posture straight and his expression composed.  

Withdrawing his gaze from the luminous screen above, he glanced at the anxious Xuan Zhen fidgeting in the distance like an ant on a hot pan. His sleeve twitched almost imperceptibly, and a faint ripple of spiritual energy flickered beneath his feet.  

He recognized all five figures displayed on the screen.
