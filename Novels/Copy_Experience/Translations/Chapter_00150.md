**Chapter 148: Bronze Divine Hall**

On an unnamed island, within the area covered by a Tier 2 formation, in a newly built courtyard, Lu Xu sat on a lounge chair, lazily basking in the sun.

A small purple sparrow hopped around on his shoulder. Streaks of purple lightning occasionally flickered across its body, and its pair of pitch-black eyes exuded a lively glow.

"Chirp chirp!"

The small purple sparrow rubbed against Lu Xu's face very affectionately, seemingly treating him as a blood relative.
