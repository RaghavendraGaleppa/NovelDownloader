# Chapter 264: The Matchmaking Pouch, This Is All for Me (1/2)

In the Azure Luan Clan territory.

The night was serene, with the Great Yin moon hanging high in the sky.

The young girl's wings, as delicate as silk spun from jade, were dyed by the moonlight into a swaying, frost-like luster.

She sat on the branch of the tallest tree in the clan territory, her long legs gently swaying as she held the big-headed doll in her arms.

She seemed to be... lost in thought?

Ying Bing looked at the moon, her eyes vacant, yet she appeared more and more like a flawless jade statue.

Before she could collect her thoughts, the branch sank slightly. The newcomer... was the blue-clothed少女.

After spending many days together, Ying Bing had learned her name.

Luan Qing.

*Jingle jingle—*

Luan Qing meant:

"We're setting out. The Old Patriarch sent me to inform you."

"Mm, thank you."

Ying Bing nodded.

But Luan Qing didn't leave. She stood there with a hesitant expression, wanting to say something but feeling shy, burying her head in her own pair of wings.

"Is there something you need my help with?"

Ying Bing's clear and crisp voice seemed to carry a bit more gentleness than usual?

Luan Qing hesitated for a moment, then shook the bell with a strange frequency.

It was intermittent. If Ying Bing hadn't learned some music theory during this time, she might not have been able to understand.

"Could you please help me hang this on the Matchmaking Tree at the Empty Void Temple?"

Luan Qing's face was filled with hope:

"I heard that as long as you hang your wish on the Matchmaking Tree, your fated person will appear soon. By then, I'll be able to speak."

"Alright..."

Ying Bing reached out to take it, only to find that Luan Qing had given her two pouches.

Luan Qing shook the bell with a smile:

"You already have a fated person, but hanging the pouches on the Matchmaking Tree can still bless you with happiness and fulfillment..."

"....."

Ying Bing fell silent for a moment before opening her mouth. Before she could speak—

"Xiao Bing'er! Where are you? We're setting out!"

Shang Qin's voice came from below.

"Coming."

Ying Bing lowered her eyes for a moment. Her fair, slender fingers ultimately clenched the pouches in her palm and placed them inside the big-headed doll.

The young girl spread her wings and leaped into the night sky.

.......

At the same time.

The fish bone boat cut through the waves, creating a splashing sound that startled the unknown beasts in the forest, scattering them in all directions.

"Long time no see."

Li Mo sat on the eye socket of the fish head, gazing at the familiarly enormous moon. The Great Yin Divine Mark on his Dan-tian Little Person faintly glimmered.

He had been staying in the Black Water Marshes, the territory of the Ming蛟 Clan, for the past few days. Every day was either cloudy or rainy, and it had been a long time since he last saw the moon.

The Great Yin was cold and quiet, just as it had always been.

It was like the moonlight that had gazed down at him countless times in the past.

Hmm.

Little Li was lost in thought, but it didn't affect his observation of the Great Yin Divine Intent in the slightest.

The Great Yin in his Little World also hung high, seemingly creating some kind of connection with the moon in the outside world.

Fully automatic cleansing of his Intent Soul, observing the Divine Intent.

"Lord Tianjiao?"

"Li... Mo?"

A voice suddenly came from behind, startling the still-dazed Li Mo.

He turned around and saw it was Murong Xiao and Wu Dameng.

"You guys aren't sleeping in the middle of the night? And you walk without a sound, you almost scared this蛟 to death."

Murong Xiao rubbed his shiny bald head:

"I thought you could see me, Brother Li."

"I was cultivating just now, with full concentration. Brother Murong, is this your first day knowing that I'm very serious when I cultivate?"

Li Mo spoke righteously, then cleared his throat and asked:

"What's up?"

"I'm hungry."

Murong Xiao hadn't eaten dinner again tonight.

He was a vegetarian, but the only vegetarian food in the Ming蛟 Clan was the seaweed used for picking teeth.

"Oh, I happened to pick some wild leeks. We can roast them to eat."

"Is that appropriate?"

"Mm... you're right, Buddhists seem to consider leeks a type of meat..."

"No, Brother Li, this is the first day of my vow of abstinence. It's crucial. I absolutely cannot fail again."

---

"....."

In the end, Murong Xiao still ate the roasted leeks. The main reason wasn't just that the roasted leeks were too fragrant.

It was also because Li Mo told him that since today was the first day, eating leeks would still make tomorrow the first day. The number of days he abstained wouldn't decrease.

It wouldn't change from the second day back to the first.

Even if the Buddha himself came, he couldn't say you had given up halfway!

Murong Xiao felt that Brother Li's words were very philosophical. He understood the Buddha so well, much more so than him.

"Truly delicious."

"You keep eating. Dameng, do you want some? With cumin and chili?"

Wu Dameng was about to nod when Li Mo shook his head and said:

"Forget it. If you eat it, it's going to rain again."

A rule for the Ming蛟 Clan traveling in the Southern Frontier: never sneeze casually.

"By the way, another passing aquatic race just now sent you a gift."

Wu Dameng ate his leeks, looking at the prodigy Little Li with envy.

"Cough, just pile it over there."

Li Mo pointed to a corner of the fish bone boat's cabin, where many gifts sent by aquatic races and even other clans were piled up, all of them marriage proposals.

The grandeur of their journey was comparable to when he was revealed as the "Wang Qi Dou Hun" on the Cloud Province's Hidden Dragon List...

"Aren't you going to take a look? You should at least consider them."

"What's there to see?"

Li Mo had no interest.

But Wu Dameng was persistent: "For example, this, the Red Crystal Coral sent by the princess of the Giant Whale Clan. Although she's a bit... large in size..."

"How large?"

"Her true form is... a hundred zhang or so?"

"How much?"

"Uh... how about you take a look at this one from the Sea King Squid Clan..."

There were more than just the Ming蛟 Clan among the powerful aquatic races heading to the Empty Void Temple.

There were even some who traveled all the way from the Southern Sea, and they had naturally heard of the Tianjiao's deeds. Moreover, the Ming蛟 Clan was different from certain other clans.

Some clans, to ensure the purity of their bloodline, only allowed internal marriages.

Even more extreme, like the Azure Luan Clan, the entire clan is waiting for their fated one.

But the Ming蛟 Clan has never been too strict about the species of their partner.

This can be seen just from Brother Murong, who is sitting there eating leeks.

"Dameng, tell me the truth. Did you accept bribes?"

Li Mo said impatiently, "The ones you're recommending are all huge."

"I think a male should be paired with a powerful female. The humans call this... 'eating soft rice'? Eating soft rice is so comfortable. Lord Li, I'm completely thinking of what's best for you."

Wu Dameng said this as if it were the most natural thing in the world.

No wonder he was able to become an actor蛟...

Li Mo fell silent for a moment:

"Where did you learn this stuff?"

"There are storytellers by the river in Nanguan City all the time. I often hear a story called 'The Overbearing Empress Falls in Love with Me'. It makes a lot of sense."

"You should listen to fewer storytellers. That's all science fiction. Do you understand science fiction? It's as ridiculous as a Ming蛟 sneezing without it raining."

"For real..."

"Hm?"

Li Mo saw Wu Dameng, who after his transformation was burly and bore the fierce, intimidating features of the蛟 race, showing a tendency to become Wu Dameng (cute version).

His fist subconsciously clenched. How strange.

The next morning.

In the distance, a grand ancient temple could already be seen atop a mountain, seemingly emitting a holy light from within the clouds and mist, with an incredibly imposing aura.

The Empty Void Temple.

And Little Li, after getting off the fish bone boat, also encountered a huge problem.

He hadn't even gotten off the boat yet.

From afar, he saw a large crowd of aquatic races gathered in front of the dock. Most of them were women with aquatic features, dressed up exquisitely, and carrying precious items from their clans.

Some of them weren't even here to compete for the Divine Jade from Beyond the Heavens at the Empty Void Temple.

So what were they here for?

Of course, it was for the True Dragon bloodline!

Even if it was just a trace!

Even if it might be very thin!

But as long as it exists, there's a possibility of returning to the ancestor in the future, a possibility of a fish leaping through the Dragon Gate!

The scene was extremely grand.

Tianjiao Little Li leaned back in a tactical maneuver, taking a slight breath of cold air.

"坏了, it's all for me."
