# Chapter 199: Ambush

Whoosh!

The bowstring of the Divine Wind Bow drew taut into a full moon before snapping back violently. A silver arrow streaked through the night sky like a shooting star, instantly exploding the head of a Shadowfang Direwolf!

Dark crimson yin energy immediately dispersed as the splattered blood—black as ink—oozed thick, viscous tendrils of shadowy matter.
