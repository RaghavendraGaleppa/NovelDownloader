# Chapter 194: The Rising Prestige of the Divine Martial Emperor

Dou Changsheng frowned slightly.

This Gao Yangming was no ordinary figure. Though he had only achieved the Heavenly Realm a few centuries ago, he had already broken into the top ten of the Heavenly Rankings—an almost inconceivable feat. Merely making it onto the Heavenly Rankings was extraordinary enough, let alone securing the eighth position. 

Yet this wasn't his limit. In the future, he might even break into the top five—or perhaps claim the very top spot.
