# Chapter 103: The Path to Inner Sect Promotion  

The main hall of Qinglu Sect was filled with an air of tension.  

Gao Yunrui and the other high-ranking elders of Qinglu Sect had gathered, yet none wore a pleasant expression. Instead, their faces were shadowed by deep concern—all due to the disastrous outcome of the recent True Disciple Trials.  

The trials had been ambushed by an unknown force, resulting in heavy casualties among the sect’s most promising disciples.
