# Chapter 488: The Banquet Begins  

The two female immortals would not easily admit guilt. In their understanding, they had merely picked a few wildflowers. Moreover, there was no stone tablet forbidding the touching of plants here, suggesting the celestial court did not consider it a serious matter.  

"This isn’t stealing immortal herbs—why are you making such a fuss, Taoist?" the younger sister snapped impatiently.
