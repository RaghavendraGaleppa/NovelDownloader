# Chapter 152: Hall of the Underworld, the White Bone Holy Mother

The sky was just beginning to lighten at dawn.

Dou Changsheng watched as the morning sun rose, casting countless rays of light across the land. 

Shifting his gaze slightly downward, he surveyed the vast earth below. Spotting a village in the distance, he patted his wooden donkey and changed direction, heading straight for the settlement. As his vision drew nearer, the layout of the village became increasingly clear.
