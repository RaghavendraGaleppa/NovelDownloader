# Chapter 306: The Lake Island – Ghost Cultivator Battles the Blademistress  

The surface of Mirror Lake rippled as a small boat drifted across its waters.  

At the stern sat a ferryman, while at the bow stood a woman clad in coarse hemp robes.  

"Gaze into the lake," the ferryman murmured. "The reflection you see is your soul. Be careful not to lose it—and beware lest it drags you down into the underworld."  

---  

Xiao Ji surveyed the devastated grove on the lake island, where remnants of spiritual techniques still scorched the air, leaving an oppressive tension in their wake.  

The vegetation of the Netherworld was not only bizarre but also several times more resilient than ordinary trees. The fact that a cultivator could annihilate miles of such foliage with their techniques spoke volumes of their formidable power.
