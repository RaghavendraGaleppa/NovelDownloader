# Chapter 151: This Assassin Is a Bit Cold  

Song Yuanbao's words left Xiao Ji deep in thought for a while before he headed to the archives to search for relevant records.  

Both the Qingyin Temple and the Green Lotus Villa were forces that thrived within the mortal realm, so there were written records available for reference.  

After reviewing the documents and learning more about the invitation letter from Song Yuanbao, Xiao Ji returned to the Primordial Spirit Temple to rest.  

At present, there were three known fragments of the Glazed Immortal Lantern, with two located in the Qingyin Temple of the Dragon City.  

Xiao Ji's first consideration was to retrieve those first.
