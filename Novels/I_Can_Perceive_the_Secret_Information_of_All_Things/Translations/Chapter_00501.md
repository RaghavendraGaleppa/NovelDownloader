# Chapter 501: Divine Horse Valley, Lord of Ten Thousand Beasts

With the Nine Treasures in his possession, Xiao Ji felt at ease no matter where he went. No matter who approached, he dared to kick them twice if necessary.

As he traveled with Yu Sanxian toward the Divine Horse Valley—the lair of the Lord of Ten Thousand Beasts—Xiao Ji often took out the Nine Treasure Stone Tablet to admire it. In the words of his past life, this was the latest version of a treasure, wielding the flawless power of the Nine Treasures.
