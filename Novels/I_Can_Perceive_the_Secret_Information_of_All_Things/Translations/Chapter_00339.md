# Chapter 339: The Bone Inn

Many who venture into the Untamed Lands are either desperate souls with nowhere left to turn or treasure hunters seeking fortune—all of them cultivators living on the edge. Among them, one finds worldly-wise ruthless individuals alongside fools who don't know their own limits...

Then there's another, more peculiar kind—those who specialize in doing business with these desperate souls. They choose to operate at the crossroads...
