# Chapter 154: Brain Teasers

The martial artist, full of malicious intent, wanted to play brain teasers with Xiao Ji—a thinly veiled attempt to make him stumble into breaking the rules.

He had picked the wrong person.

Before long, the martial artist's mind would be the one unable to bend.

"Go ahead, I'm best at brain teasers. My mind has always been quite flexible," Xiao Ji set down his chopsticks and replied seriously.

"Let me ask you first—what place is this?" the martial artist inquired.

Yet another question designed to trap the unwary.
