# Chapter 205: The Spirit Conch

The white flying shuttle streaked across the sky.

This top-grade spiritual artifact shuttle, imbued with seventy-two layers of restrictions, was truly exceptional.

As its master, Jin Guanli stood at the forefront of the shuttle with one hand clasped behind his back and the other forming a seal, exuding an air of mastery as he steered the vessel over the barren white mountains below.
