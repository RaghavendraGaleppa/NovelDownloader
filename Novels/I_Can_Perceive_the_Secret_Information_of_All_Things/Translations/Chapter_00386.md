# Chapter 386: The Third Princess's Farewell

The Yuanshen Temple completed its transformation from a secret realm to a blessed land. That day, the entire Longxiang County was enveloped in an auspicious aura.

This blessed land in the mortal world was truly one of a kind.

Throughout countless years in the cultivation world, secret realms had always been the norm. Only immortals from the celestial realm constructed cave heavens and blessed lands, and even those were relatively rare in number.
