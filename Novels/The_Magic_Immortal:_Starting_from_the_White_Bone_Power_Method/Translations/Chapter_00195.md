# Chapter 195: The Moon Illuminates White Mountain

Three months later.

A cold moon hung high in the sky, its silvery light draping over the land like a frosty veil.

At the mouth of a cave five hundred zhang up a sheer cliff face, Wang Chan sat cross-legged in meditation.

In the distance, layer upon layer of verdant peaks rose and fell like the undulating back of a slumbering dragon, their ends lost beyond the veil of misty haze. 

For a newcomer like him, this nightscape held a peculiar charm.
