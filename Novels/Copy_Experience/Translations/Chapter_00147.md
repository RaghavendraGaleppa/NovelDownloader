# Chapter 145: Wei Mingyang Has Arrived

The strange phenomenon triggered by the formation of the Golden Core quickly subsided. The dark clouds in the sky dispersed, and the vast spiritual energy that had gathered also vanished into thin air. The clear blue sky once again returned to its vast, cloudless expanse.

Under that blue canopy, a figure stood quietly, emanating a blazing light. Gradually, this light receded, sinking into the figure. However, as the light dissipated, a terrifying aura spread out, with that person as its center.
