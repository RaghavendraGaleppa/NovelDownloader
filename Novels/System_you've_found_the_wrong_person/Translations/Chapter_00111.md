# Chapter 111: I'm Just a 200-Month-Old Baby

Late at night.

A bright moon hung high in the starry sky. Its hazy glow, like a thin veil of gauze, draped gently over the land.

Before the grand gates of the Prime Minister's residence, lanterns hung high, their light pushing back the darkness and illuminating the entrance as bright as day. A squad of armored guards stood watch, spears in hand.
