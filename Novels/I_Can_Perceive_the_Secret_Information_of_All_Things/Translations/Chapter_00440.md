# Chapter 440: The Heavenly Tribulation Arrives  

Zhao Jiuzhou had successfully developed a magical artifact capable of resisting heavenly tribulation, though the cost was substantial—nearly half of Tianzhu Mountain's accumulated wealth had been spent, primarily due to the exorbitant price of the materials required.  

Yet, in Xiao Ji's eyes, it was well worth it. After all, this might be the only portable artifact in existence capable of withstanding heavenly tribulation—though its effectiveness still remained...
