Although Ying Bing was puzzled in her heart, she still answered the questions on the paper from the shallow to the profound.

"Sword techniques and sword arts are essentially the same thing, nothing more than precision, flexibility, and making them your own. If you practice a superior sword method to the level of perfection, you fall into this category, which is considered passable."

"However, for those who truly possess Sword Qi, Sword Momentum, Sword Intent, and the Sword Path, sword techniques are merely the experience they summarized on their way to the Sword Path."

They were summarized by those geniuses who truly reached the origin.

Li Mo nodded and took out paper and pen.

"What are you doing?"

"Hmm, I'm taking notes."

Li Mo smiled and began to write rapidly. It was still simple now; if it got deeper later, he was afraid he wouldn't be able to remember it all.

Ying Bing felt a little confused. In her past and present lives, she had discussed the Dao with others, but this was the first time someone took notes while discussing. After all, these weren't things to be memorized by rote; those who understood would grasp it instantly.

"Sword Qi, Sword Momentum, and Sword Intent are indeed three different paths, yet they lead to the same destination. Let's start with Sword Qi. Before the Great Yu, there was the Great Shang. Of course, Li Mo wasn't just listening seriously; his hand was also writing non-stop.

"Did you understand everything I just said?"

As a genius capable of asking such questions, she thought that after explaining to this extent, he should have understood seventy to eighty percent.

"I understand!" Li Mo nodded confidently.

"Then tell me your insights."

Ying Bing's eyes, like wind and snow, stared directly into Li Mo's eyes, as if waiting for good news.

"Uh..."

Li Mo's expression froze, his smile gradually disappeared, and he fell into thought. *Was I a bit... too carried away just now? How could I have forgotten that Ice Cube would also test me? Oh, right, it was a discussion. Ice Cube talked for so long; I can't just say nothing.*

"One, limited to technique. The hand has the sword, but the heart has no sword. A common man's rage, blood splatters five steps."

"The second, the hand has no sword, the heart holds the sword. What is practiced is the unity of man and sword, capable of using all things as swords."

"The third, the hand has no sword, the heart also has no sword. At that time, your very existence is the Sword Path."

The calm tone fell. The boy who spoke freely and eloquently seemed to care little about the evaluations of others, merely stating the Sword Path he understood.

Opposite him, the jade-like beauty drinking tea had an expression as if the ice and snow were melting, showing a hint of emotion.

After a long while, she parted her lips:

"Good."

"You taught well."

"No, you are right. The great Dao is simple; simplifying complexity is the correct principle."

"You flatter me."

Li Mo smiled on the surface, but he wiped a huge sweat in his heart. Fortunately, he used to secretly read novels during class in his previous life. His teacher caught him back then and said he wouldn't have a future this way. Look, it came in handy perfectly!

However, seeing Ice Cube moved, with a hint of possibly asking another question, Little Li hurriedly asked first:

"If one only practices sword techniques, can one touch the Sword Path?"

Upon hearing this, Ying Bing pondered. After thinking it over, she nodded:

"Yes, but also no."

"What do you mean by 'yes'?"

Li Mo thought to himself, Ice Cube doesn't seem like someone who speaks in riddles.

"Technique approaches the Dao. Theoretically, this is indeed possible."

"But there are so many sword methods in the world. A normal person spends their entire life learning, and what they acquire is just a ladleful from the ocean. They die of old age before achieving it, so no one can do it, unless... forget it, it's meaningless to dwell on this."

Ying Bing had almost said that only the legendary 'Immortals' could live as long as heaven and earth, having enough time to achieve 'technique approaches the Dao.' But the existence of Immortals is itself even more ethereal and unfathomable than technique approaching the Dao.

"I see."
