# Chapter 168: Lin Musheng Bids Farewell to Yuanshen Temple, Third Princess Takes Her First Steps in the Dao at the Kitchen

Under Xiao Ji's guidance, Lin Musheng achieved Foundation Establishment and mastered the Heart Sword simultaneously.  

The energy unleashed upon successful Foundation Establishment was so immense that even Xiao Ji, a Golden Core cultivator, had his robes billowing wildly and his facial features slightly distorted by the force. As for weaker creatures nearby, the impact was even more overwhelming.  

Lin Musheng's released spiritual energy intertwined with yang-fire qi, sweeping across the surroundings. The snow covering the wilderness was instantly cleared, melting upon contact, while massive boulders shattered into countless fragments.
