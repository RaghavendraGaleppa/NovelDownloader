# Chapter 121: Elder, You Don't Seem Quite Human

On the outskirts of Changzhou, an elderly man stood with sparse hair, patches of scalp clearly visible. He was clearly balding, and it wouldn't be long before he lost all his hair.

His face twisted with indignation as he raised a trembling finger toward Dou Changsheng, poised to unleash a torrent of curses. Yet at the last moment, he swallowed the vulgar words back down his throat.
