# Chapter 204: Heavenly Net Fruit

"Fellow Daoist Wang, shall we join forces to venture into Heavenly Wind Mountain?"

The entrance to Heavenly Wind Mountain stretched over a hundred miles, with countless points of entry. The five cultivators had chosen a secluded valley, where they set up defensive formations to serve as a temporary resting place.

As night fell, Lady Lu brewed spiritual tea and prepared an exquisite spread of spirit-nourishing delicacies and fine wines.
