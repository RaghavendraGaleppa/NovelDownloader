# Chapter 272: Divine Armory Valley Secret Realm

Descending the mountain.

After leaving Tianzhu Mountain, Xiao Ji and Zhao Jiuzhou traveled northwest together. The Divine Armory Valley lay beyond the borders of the Great Li Kingdom, hidden within an unclaimed forest.

Whether the location was accurate or not could only be confirmed by going there in person. Matters that even the most gossipy cultivators dared not casually report on were mostly shrouded in mystery.
