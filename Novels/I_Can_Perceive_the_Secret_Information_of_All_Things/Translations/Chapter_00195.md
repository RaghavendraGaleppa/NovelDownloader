# Chapter 195: They Don't Dare Challenge Me to a Duel

With a single hand-chop, Xiao Ji severed the head of the Valley Seven Fiends' leader.

Song Yuanbao stared at the head on the ground, then at the still-standing body, before finally looking back at Xiao Ji.

Xiao Ji extended a finger and pushed the old troupe leader's body over. The iron tobacco pipe clattered loudly as it fell from the lifeless hand and struck the ground.

"Actually, I didn't mean to take his head off. I'm not that cruel," he explained.

No explanation was needed.
