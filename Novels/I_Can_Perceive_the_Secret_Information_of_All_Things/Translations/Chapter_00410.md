# Chapter 410: The Truth

Xiao Ji instructed Fairy Feiliu and her close companion to return first, planning to regroup with them once he found clues to track down the culprits. 

On the surface, this appeared to be a trivial matter. It seemed peculiar that a supreme cultivator at the Mahayana stage would trouble himself with the affairs of mere book peddlers within the Dao City. Even if it concerned his reputation, such involvement seemed beneath his stature.
