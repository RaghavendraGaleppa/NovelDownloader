# Chapter 156: Without Reaching Foundation Establishment, One Remains an Ant  

"Eh? Feng-di [1], isn't that your wife over there? She's talking to Wang Chan!" Another direct descendant of the Bai family, who had once traveled to the Guanlan Kingdom with them, pointed at the woman in the green dress in surprise. "They seem quite familiar. Could it be that you know Wang Chan as well?"  

"I believe Mei-er [2] mentioned him before," Bai Shaofeng replied with a slight frown.  

---  
[1] "Feng-di" - A term of address combining the character for "phoenix/wind" (风) with "di" (弟), meaning "younger brother." It suggests familiarity and closeness, likely used by an older family member or close friend.  
[2] "Mei-er" - An affectionate nickname for Bai Shaofeng's wife, with "Mei" (梅) meaning "plum blossom" and the suffix "-er" (儿) denoting endearment.
