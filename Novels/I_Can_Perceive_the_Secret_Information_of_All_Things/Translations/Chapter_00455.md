# Chapter 455: The Snow of That Year, The People of That Year  

Xiao Ji arrived at Springwater City with the sole intention of reaping the final benefits. Yet, after glimpsing into the fates of several individuals, he saw reflections of his own humble past—his former life of insignificance and the frailty he had felt when he first arrived at the Origin Temple.  

From Dog Leftover to the old beggar, from Liu Feng to Wang Jiusheng—these four were nothing more than pawns in fate's cruel game.
