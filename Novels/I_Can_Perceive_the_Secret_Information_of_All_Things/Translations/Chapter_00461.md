# Chapter 461: The Final Situation

Yun Kaitian appeared.

"You're getting worse and worse. I sent you to kill a beggar, yet you came here to deal with some insignificant nobody," Yun Kaitian said with disdain, his disciples standing proudly behind him as they looked at Yun Xue.

"I just wanted to avenge my brother," Yun Xue insisted, believing she had done nothing wrong.

Yun Kaitian ignored Yun Xue's words.
