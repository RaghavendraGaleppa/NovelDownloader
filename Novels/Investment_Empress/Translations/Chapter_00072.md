## Chapter 72: The Nine Peaks Grand Competition Begins, Old Xiao's First Battle

The heavens smiled upon them; today was a sunny day. Below Qingyuan Main Peak, various convoys streamed in, a magnificent torrent flowing into the sea. Deacons were everywhere, maintaining order. It was a grand spectacle, showcasing Qingyuan Sect's prestige as the foremost of Purple Sun Prefecture's three major sects.

"So lively?" Li Mo lifted the carriage curtain, surveying the scene outside. He had only ventured down the mountain once for a mission, hardly a true foray into the Jianghu.

Old Ba, driving the carriage, chuckled, "Our Qingyuan Sect's Nine Peaks Grand Competition is a stage for the next generation of our sect to display their strength. Naturally, smaller sects from the vicinity, as well as the other two major sects, will send people to observe the ceremony."

"I see." Li Mo nodded, now understanding the reasons for the Grand Competition's bustle. The new replace the old; the new generation would sooner or later become the mainstays of the sect, representing its future strength. Strength dictated status, and how other sects would position themselves in the future. Moreover, not long ago, a magnificent celestial phenomenon had manifested above Qingyuan Sect, illuminating the heavens. Many had come with the intention of probing its true nature.

As Old Ba spoke, he pointed ahead, "That's the Burning Jade Forest contingent. Leading them is one of their six Chief Elders." Li Mo followed his gaze. The Burning Jade Forest hadn't sent many people, just a dozen or so, but their presence was significant. Their disciples were all exceptionally dressed, armed with renowned weapons, clearly distinguishing themselves from the surrounding minor factions. A white-robed disciple stood at the very front, hands clasped behind his back, a faint arrogance in his eyes.

"Burning Jade Forest is located to the west of Purple Sun Prefecture, and a part of their territory borders the Central Divine Continent, which is why some of their disciples have extraordinary backgrounds," Old Ba explained upon seeing this.

"And over there?" Li Mo asked again.

Old Ba glanced over, chuckling, "That's the Red Whale Gang. They're the largest gang in Purple Sun Prefecture, and quite renowned even in the Eastern Barren Domain. On the surface, their strength is at the bottom among the three major sects, but it's a mixed bag of dragons and fish, and their waters run very deep."

The Red Whale Gang's side was much livelier. They had connections, direct or indirect, with many minor sects, inevitably leading to a flurry of greetings and pleasantries. Hearing this, Li Mo nodded slightly. He squinted. The leader was a Red Whale Gang helmsman, but what caught Li Mo's attention wasn't him, but the blood-robed youth beside him. This youth gave him a strange feeling. But before he could activate his Heaven's Mandate Divine Eye, his sight was blocked by a familiar figure: the Purple Sun Prefecture Lord. The Lord, whom he had met once before, began conversing with the leading helmsman.

The carriage moved on. Before long, they arrived at the mid-slope of Qingyuan Main Peak.

"Go get 'em, my precious disciple," Shang Wu said, stifling a yawn as she rose and gracefully floated onto the high platform. The Peak Master Elders were already seated there, with a few extra seats reserved for observers from other sects.

Shangguan Wencang sat in the center, his gaze sweeping over the scene. Upon seeing Ying Bing, a look of profound expectation flashed in his eyes. It wasn't just the Sect Master's gaze. Many pairs of eyes in the arena also turned towards her at this moment. Shangguan Wencang's only disciple, the acknowledged successor to the Qingyuan Sect Master position, and a person of outstanding talent among her peers. With numerous enviable halos surrounding her, combined with a reclusive, high-mountain snow lotus-like temperament, it was difficult for her to be ignored.

At this moment, Sect Master Qingyuan's voice resounded. "Today marks the Nine Peaks Grand Competition, so I will keep my words brief. Fellow colleagues, esteemed heroes, our Qingyuan Sect's competition this time focuses on several key points..."
