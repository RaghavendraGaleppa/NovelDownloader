# Chapter 178: The Scripture of Dark Bliss

Even the most ignorant person could tell something was amiss at Qingyin Temple when so many officers from the Evil-Suppression Bureau appeared.

"I remember one day when I was lying there, waiting to fight other adult beggars for porridge at dawn," Nail recounted. "Before daybreak, a golden light shot out from the temple, flashing southeastward. After that day, Qingyin Temple stopped giving out porridge. That's why I ended up begging in the Beggars' Sect territory. I'm telling the truth."
