# Chapter 220: The Chicken Demon

Xiao Ji had no intention of showing off. He only had that one sword, and to fly by sword, he had to summon it, enlarge it, and then step onto it—this was the most basic technique of sword flight.

He wanted everyone to hurry to the dilapidated mountain temple, quickly survey the surroundings, identify any gaps or oversights, and then head northeast in pursuit, hoping to find traces of Lord Qin Liang as soon as possible.

Unlike kidnappers who hold hostages for ransom, evil spirits that abduct people don't wait for payment—they act with far more sinister intentions.
