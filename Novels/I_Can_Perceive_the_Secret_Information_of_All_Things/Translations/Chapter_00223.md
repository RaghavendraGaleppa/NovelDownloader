# Chapter 223: National Crisis

The Compassionate Deliverance Palm struck out, annihilating the flock of avian demons in an instant while completely obliterating their nest ahead. From above, one could only see a long stretch of barren land—a testament to the devastating power of that single palm strike.

The Rooster King had never imagined its end would come so abruptly. In its final moments, it didn't even get the chance to lay one last egg. Its grand ambitions—to unite the avian realm, establish a kingdom of birds, and reign supreme over humanity—had barely taken form before being extinguished in the blink of an eye.
