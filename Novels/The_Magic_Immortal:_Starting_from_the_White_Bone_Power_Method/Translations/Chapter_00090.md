# Chapter 90: Azure Origin Robe

Torrential rain poured down the alleyway, where a solitary figure stood indistinct in the downpour.

She wore a deep violet gown, her waist-length hair tied loosely with a white jade ribbon. A protective barrier of magical energy shielded her from the rain as she stood gracefully before the entrance to Courtyard Sixty-Nine. Her slender waist and delicate, refined features gave her an air of quiet elegance.
