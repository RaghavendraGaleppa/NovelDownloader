# Chapter 105: Beauty's Stratagem

The Cloud Tower teahouse stood four stories tall, bustling with patrons and alive with activity. Yet neither the lively atmosphere within nor the vibrant streets below could capture Mo Jingchun's attention as she stood by the window.

Two hours had passed since Wei Yulan's departure, and still there was no sign of her return. Could something have gone wrong? A growing unease settled in Mo Jingchun's heart.
