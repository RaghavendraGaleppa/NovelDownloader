# Chapter 466: Side Story - The Undying Sword Immortal Wan Jiayi's Epiphany  

The cries of a six- or seven-year-old boy disturbed the neighbors and unsettled the autumn air.  

People gathered in the dilapidated courtyard, whispering among themselves, occasionally casting pitying glances at the child.  

"Poor little one," someone murmured. "No family left in this world, all alone. What will become of him now?"
