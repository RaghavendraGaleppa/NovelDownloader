# Chapter 77: Hitching a Ride on the Flying Boat

Standing in the post station square of the Little Black River market, he gazed at the towering building in the distance. The powerful fluctuations of the formation array could be distinctly felt, and the faintly discernible pressure emanating from it caused him to instinctively take half a step back.

That oppressive aura was truly terrifying.

Though not as formidable as the second-tier formation array in the black market, it was undoubtedly of the same caliber.
