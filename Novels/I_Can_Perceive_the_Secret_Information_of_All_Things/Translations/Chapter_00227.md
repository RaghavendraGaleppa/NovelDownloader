# Chapter 227: The Weak Water Sect's Angler

Having lived two lives, Xiao Ji understood the importance of perspective-taking. He often put himself in others' positions and realized that despite his attempts to conceal it, his behavior still appeared undeniably peculiar.

Some actions simply defied explanation through words. After all, the insights he gained through perception couldn't withstand scrutiny—a few probing questions would leave him speechless. In the end, he usually resorted to describing his uniqueness as mere intuition.

This situation was quite understandable. It was like holding a cucumber in your hand and claiming it was secondhand—
