# Chapter 16: Display of Dominance

"Steward Sun is currently inside. Shall I go and announce your arrival?" Liu Jun asked in a low voice, following beside Chen Fei inside the North City Medical Hall.

Ever since he heard that a new Deputy Steward was coming to the North City Medical Hall, Liu Jun had been paying close attention. As a marginalized figure in the medical hall, only by latching onto someone powerful could he hope to advance. A new Deputy Steward, fresh to the place, would surely be unfamiliar with many things. Approaching them now would undoubtedly lead to closeness, perhaps even becoming their trusted subordinate.

"No need," Chen Fei shook his head. "We can wait until Steward Sun is free."

Chen Fei shook his head. Sun Shu clearly knew he was coming today, yet didn't bother to come out, likely indicating either a dislike for him, the 'parachute' appointment, or simply indifference. Either way, it represented Sun Shu's attitude. Chen Fei didn't care. Upon arriving at the North City Medical Hall, he had no intention of digging up any dirt on Sun Shu. His only goal was to refine pills peacefully, earn good money, and improve his cultivation. As for the 'pie in the sky' promised by the Zhang family, Chen Fei had absolutely no intention of biting into it.

"Does Steward Sun have any hobbies?" Chen Fei asked, after walking a round in the alchemy room, turning to Liu Jun.

"Steward Sun likes to kick feet," Liu Jun said, his expression a little strange.

"Kick feet?"

Compared to before, Chen Fei had made significant progress in both flexibility and speed. He had also witnessed some Skin-Refining Realm martial artists running, and with a slight comparison, he realized his movement technique was far superior.
