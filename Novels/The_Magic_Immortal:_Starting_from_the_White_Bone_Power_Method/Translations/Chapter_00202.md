# Chapter 202: Two Fists to Death

Each of them was a mid-grade second-tier beast, exuding a fierce white demonic aura.  

Wang Chan glanced back and immediately took off running.  

Against one, he was confident he could win.  

But seven? That would drain him completely.  

If surrounded, he'd be in grave danger.  

And right now, his magic power hadn't even recovered yet.
