# Chapter 494: Where Have the Immortal Emperors Gone?

During their exploration of the Celestial Court ruins, the gathered immortals were met with a desolate scene. The once-bustling Celestial Court, which had previously been guarded by celestial soldiers at every turn, now stood completely devoid of life.

At first, they thought nothing of it, treating it merely as an unusual sight to behold. It wasn't until Xiao Ji discovered traces of battle that some of the more excitable immortals finally calmed down.
