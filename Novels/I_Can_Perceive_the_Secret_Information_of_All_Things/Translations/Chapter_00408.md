# Chapter 408: The Long Journey with Daoist Xiao

The Grand Priest finally agreed.

After all, the tribe had long forgotten about those three bells. Moreover, their current plight of being hunted down was precisely because of those artifacts. If they didn't give them to Daoist Xiao now, they would still face relentless pursuit upon returning to the human realm.

"These cursed objects shall be yours, Daoist Xiao." Gritting his teeth, the Grand Priest made the decision to hand them over.
