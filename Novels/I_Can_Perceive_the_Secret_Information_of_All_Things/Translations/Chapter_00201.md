# Chapter 201: Everyone Has Been Anxious Lately

Sage Hand Liu narrowed his eyes slightly as he looked at the woman and asked slowly, "What brings you here?"

"Because... many strange things have been happening in Huangbai Town," the woman replied. "People have been disappearing without a trace, dying inexplicably, and even falling gravely ill for no apparent reason. I've been investigating in secret and discovered that all these incidents are connected to your Revival Clinic. That's why I'm anxious."

"So you must be Huang Dazhuang, the female constable of Huangbai Town," Sage Hand Liu remarked, unfazed.
