# Chapter 126: The Key to Forming the Core

Ancient City of Tai.

Xiao Ji had been here for several days now.

He had taken all the accumulated items he couldn't use himself and placed them in the auction house. For things the auction house wouldn't accept—like the blood-boosting elixirs confiscated from Young Master Wuyou or the peculiar yet high-quality magical artifacts found in the Flower-Crushing Palace—he simply sold them at low prices to vendors on Treasure Hunters' Street.

Setting up his own stall never crossed his mind. Since he wasn't a permanent resident of Ancient City of Tai, he couldn't afford to waste time...
