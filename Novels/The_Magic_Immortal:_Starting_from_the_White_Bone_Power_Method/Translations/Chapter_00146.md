# Chapter 146: Profound Netherbone Wood

The three figures in white robes gradually disappeared into the distance.

Yunbai Alley instantly fell into silence.

The wind grew restless, tousling hair and making black robes flap loudly. 

Thick tendrils of white mist coiled between the towering parasol-like yunbai trees, clinging to faces with an icy chill.

Though the afternoon sun shone brightly, its warmth seemed unable to penetrate the gloom that had settled over the alley.
