**Chapter 37: Grand Lunar Metamorphosis Corpse Disentanglement True Method**

“This *Grand Lunar Metamorphosis Corpse Disentanglement True Method* is a bit difficult.”

After a night in seclusion, Lu Yang frowned, staring at the divine ability manual before him. “A Grand Divine Ability is something only those with great magical power can wield. It represents the pinnacle of divine abilities.”

What is known as a Grand Divine Ability is not actually a rank of divine ability, but rather a method of application. Simply put, it involves combining several compatible superior divine abilities to unleash dozens of times their original power.

The *Grand Lunar Metamorphosis Corpse Disentanglement True Method* is precisely this. To master this Grand Divine Ability, one must first learn three superior divine abilities: the Heavenly Demon Blood-Transforming Divine Light, the Profound Yin Form-Seizing Grand Method, and the Supreme Clarity Edict Scroll. Each of these three divine abilities is extremely difficult to cultivate, achievable only through an immense fortuitous encounter.

This is also the commonality among all Grand Divine Abilities. It’s not something you can cultivate just by wanting to; it depends on your comprehension, your luck, and your merit. Lacking any one of these will only lead to failure.

Take the first divine ability, the Heavenly Demon Blood-Transforming Divine Light. This single divine ability alone has trapped countless cultivators, as cultivating it requires flaying oneself, which is equivalent to severing one's path to immortality, rendering it meaningless even if successfully cultivated.

As for the second divine ability, the Profound Yin Form-Seizing Grand Method, it doesn't have such severe side effects. Its difficulty primarily lies in external materials. To cultivate this divine ability, one must use a “Profound Womb Grand Lunar Treasure Jade” as a medium. Without this rare treasure, no matter how high your cultivation or how good your comprehension, you cannot master it.

And the “Profound Womb Grand Lunar Treasure Jade” is exceedingly rare. How rare? A single sentence can describe it: it's not even listed in the Saint Sect's exchange catalog, meaning you can't buy it with contribution points.

Given the vastness and abundant resources of the Saint Sect, yet even they do not possess such a wonder. This shows that cultivating this divine ability entirely depends on a cultivator's fortuitous encounters and luck. Without that luck, even if you search the entire world, it will only be in vain.

Thus, Lu Yang merely glanced at it before decisively giving up and turning his attention to the third divine ability, the Supreme Clarity Edict Scroll.

However, to his dismay, while this divine ability neither required precious external materials nor carried severe side effects, it was truly too difficult. Lu Yang studied it all night with very little progress.

“It's somewhat like a formation, yet also like a talisman, but categorized as a divine ability. Whether one can learn it depends entirely on comprehension; if you can't, you just can't.”

Thinking this, Lu Yang scratched his head again, refusing to be defeated. “I've already painstakingly overcome formations, no matter how difficult they were. How long can a mere divine ability hold me back? At worst, I'll spend another twenty years; I'll master you eventually!”

The reason the *Grand Lunar Metamorphosis Corpse Disentanglement True Method* compelled Lu Yang to persist so fiercely was simply how incredibly appealing this Grand Divine Ability was; he couldn't bear to part with it.

The power of the Heavenly Demon Blood-Transforming Divine Light goes without saying, and the Profound Yin Form-Seizing Grand Method is equally overbearing. As long as one captures a person's vital qi and then condenses a “soul projection” from it, one can directly kill that person out of thin air by slaying the “soul projection,” even if they are a thousand miles apart – a sure path to death.

The Supreme Clarity Edict Scroll was even more outrageous. Once the scroll is formed, it doesn't harm the physical body or destroy the soul; it specializes in divining the karma of heaven and earth. No matter what your origins, it can clearly calculate your ancestry up to eighteen generations.

Of course, the most powerful aspect was the combination of the three divine abilities. This was also the most heart-stirring effect of the *Grand Lunar Metamorphosis Corpse Disentanglement True Method* for Lu Yang: it significantly increased a cultivator's chances of breaking through to Foundation Establishment!

For this reason alone, Lu Yang was determined to obtain this Grand Divine Ability!

Time flowed like water, years slipped by like shuttles.

In the blink of an eye, five springs had passed.

Inside the Blood-Clad Tower, Lu Yang leaned against the railing, gazing at the sky, silently circulating the “Nine Transformations Dragon Art.” With each breath, he exhaled and inhaled clouds and mist, transforming and manifesting various bizarre and wondrous phenomena.

The Eighth Transformation: Illusory Mirage Transformation!

Although the spirit vein on Skeleton Mountain was poor, Lu Yang had plenty of money and had already stockpiled a large amount of spirit stones beforehand, so his cultivation did not suffer any delays.

After five years of arduous cultivation, even though there was still no progress in cultivating the Supreme Clarity Edict Scroll, he suddenly experienced a flash of enlightenment while studying divine abilities. His true qi spontaneously broke through a barrier, and by the time he came to his senses, the Qi Seed in his Dantian had already transformed again, subtly manifesting a dragon shape.

Qi Condensation, Eighth Layer!

“Although the increase from Qi Condensation Seventh Layer to Eighth Layer wasn't as exaggerated as from Sixth to Seventh, my true qi and magical power have still nearly multiplied several times over.”

“After all, cultivation realm is the foundation!”

“What if I don't have a Grand Divine Ability? With my current strength, I can cross realms and kill even a core disciple in the mid-Qi Condensation stage!”

“Not to mention the Ninth Transformation...”

Lu Yang's heart burned with anticipation. The Ninth Transformation, “Flying Fish Transformation,” could break through bottlenecks. In other words, after he broke through to Qi Condensation Ninth Layer, he could immediately attempt to reach perfection!

“The path to immortality is within sight! This calls for a celebration!”

Thinking this, Lu Yang's mood instantly brightened, and the gloom of failing to cultivate the Supreme Clarity Edict Scroll was swept away. He then cheerfully walked out of his quiet chamber.

But before that, Lu Yang first cast the Qi Concealment Art.

The next second, his newly gained vast aura was completely restrained, returning him to the Qi Condensation Seventh Layer, just a little stronger than he was five years ago.

“Attack with three parts, keep seven parts as trump cards—you can never have too many hidden cards.”

Not long after, the Saint Sect disciples under the Blood-Clad Tower immediately swarmed over, each performing a grand bow without a word upon seeing Lu Yang.

“Congratulations, Tower Lord, on emerging from seclusion!”

“Rise.” Lu Yang waved his hand dismissively, then asked, “In the five years I was in seclusion, were there any disturbances near Skeleton Mountain?”

“Reporting, Tower Lord, none.”

An elderly Saint Sect disciple volunteered, “Only once, a disciple from the Divine Might Sect in the Northern Wilderness came to provoke us, but Fairy Feixia sent them away.”

“What about the Profound Womb Grand Lunar Treasure Jade? Any news?”

“None yet...”

After learning about the changes over the past five years, Lu Yang dismissed the crowd, intending to enter seclusion again until the Foundation Establishment opportunity during the Grand War between Righteous and Demonic Factions appeared.

However, just then, a ray of escaping light, like a colorful cloud, suddenly descended beside the Blood-Clad Tower.

“Junior Brother Lu, please grant me an audience.”

Hearing this, Lu Yang swept his spiritual sense, then quickly rose to greet her, smiling, “I apologize for inconveniencing Senior Sister to come in person. It should have been me visiting Senior Sister.”

“Junior Brother, something has happened.”

Fairy Feixia's expression was serious, and she spoke solemnly, “Recently, the Earth Observation Instrument detected that the corpse tide, which was originally supposed to erupt fifteen years from now, has advanced.”

“Right now, tens of thousands of zombies are heading towards the market city!”

“Such an anomaly, with no prior warning, must have a mastermind behind it. I suspect it's highly likely those barbarians from the Divine Might Sect in the Northern Wilderness.”

“What?”

Lu Yang frowned upon hearing this, and seeing Fairy Feixia staring intently at him, his frown deepened even further. “Senior Sister needs my assistance?”

“Precisely. Junior Brother, are you willing to venture into the corpse tide with me?”

Fairy Feixia smiled, “A corpse tide is a crisis, but also an opportunity. No matter who the mastermind is, to trigger the corpse tide early, they must be relying on some wondrous treasure.”

“If we work together, perhaps we can seize it and gain a great fortuitous encounter.”

“Even if something goes wrong and we're trapped in the corpse tide, with our cultivation, the risk isn't great!”

At this, Fairy Feixia's brows soared, radiating brilliance and appearing full of youthful vigor.

However, Lu Yang shook his head.

“My apologies, Senior Sister... I am not like Senior Sister; my strength is low, and I haven't learned many divine abilities. I only understand a few formations, so it would be better for me to stay and guard the market city.”

No matter how small the risk, it was still a risk. A gentleman does not stand under a crumbling wall. He was now on a steady path to cultivate and would certainly break through to the Qi Condensation Grand Perfection stage. Why risk his life unnecessarily?

It wasn't worth it!

His primary goal in this life was to break through to Qi Condensation Grand Perfection. If he were to act recklessly, it would be after his breakthrough; before that, he should proceed cautiously.

“Junior Brother, you're not going?” Seeing Lu Yang refuse, Fairy Feixia's delicate brows furrowed slightly. Her usually aloof demeanor revealing such a difficult expression instantly made her seem pitiful and endearing. “The immortal path is short; to achieve something, one must burn their bridges. I thought you understood this principle.”

Lu Yang's dao heart was like iron, completely unshaken. “Times have changed, Senior Sister. Please understand.”

“Junior Brother, you've changed.”

Fairy Feixia shook her head in disappointment. “Since that's the case, you may stay and guard the market city, but when the time comes, there will be no fortuitous encounters for you.”

“...”

Lu Yang smiled, offering no reply.

To be honest, he felt that Fairy Feixia didn't quite resemble a Saint Sect disciple. How should he put it? She possessed an innocence as if she hadn't been “beaten down” by the Saint Sect.

With the corpse tide ongoing, even if you gain an immense fortuitous encounter, won't you eventually have to flee back to the market city I'm guarding?

At that time, if you say there's no share of the fortuitous encounter for me? Then don't blame me for claiming you fought bravely and ultimately sacrificed yourselves on the front lines against the corpse tide...
