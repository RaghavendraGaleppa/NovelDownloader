# Chapter 99: Mo Qingchan's Repayment

After the clouds dispersed and the rain scattered, Lu Xu bathed with Mo Qingchan's service, thoroughly enjoying a comfortable and leisurely life.

Lying in the steaming pool, a delicate body, as white as ivory carvings, lay draped over him. Reaching out to touch it, it twisted smoothly, like a mermaid.

A feeling of utter comfort spread throughout his entire body.

These pleasant days filled Lu Xu with an urge to sink into them completely.

This is how immortal cultivation should be!
