# Chapter 117: Assassinating the Emperor, Betrayal!

A bolt of lightning flashed.

The dim world instantly brightened, then darkened again.

Torrential rain poured down like the heavens weeping, as if mourning Lu Shenqi.

It was all over.

There was no way to survive this tribulation.

Such a calamity would be impossible to withstand even for those who had survived two or even three heavenly tribulations.
