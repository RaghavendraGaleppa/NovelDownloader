# Chapter 384: Rebirth

One wave subsides, only for another to rise in its place.

After the twelve parasitic powerhouses of the Dao Alliance were all eradicated, an entirely new cultivation world was destined to emerge.

That day, Xiao Ji struck down Sky Path Master, freezing the man's life at the very moment before ascension. Until his dying breath, the master never managed to take that final step through the Heavenly Gate.
