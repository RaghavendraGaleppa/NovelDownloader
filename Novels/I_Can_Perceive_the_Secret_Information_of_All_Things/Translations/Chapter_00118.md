# Chapter 118: Something Big Happened

In the blink of an eye, another spring arrived—the season when all living things chased after one another.

The Yuanshen Temple was having an ordinary lunch.

They were eating stewed meat.

However, Xiao Ji's meal was different—it included boiled eggs, sweet dumplings, grapes... essentially, everything was round.

He had developed a keen interest in round objects recently.

The reason was simple: his cultivation had hit a bottleneck, a problem that had troubled him throughout the entire winter.
