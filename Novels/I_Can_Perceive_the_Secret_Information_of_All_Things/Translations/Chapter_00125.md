# Chapter 125: The Unclean Taoist Priest

Xiao Ji's involvement in the county affairs wasn't entirely without gain—at the very least, he had contributed to his hometown.

The Willow Town government office was promoted to a third-rank establishment. Three large red flowers now hung proudly above its entrance. Previously a second-rank office, it had risen one level thanks to Xiao Ji's assistance.

The town magistrate was overjoyed and personally hosted a banquet in Xiao Ji's honor. It wasn't just the magistrate who was pleased—the townsfolk were equally delighted, now having fresh material for their daily conversations with visiting outsiders.
