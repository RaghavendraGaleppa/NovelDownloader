# Chapter 287: The Wolf Demon with a Story and Its Final Journey

In the Trial Forest, three furtive creatures moved through the undergrowth. Upon closer inspection, they revealed themselves to be a tiger, a leopard, and a wolf—all walking upright with distinctly human-like postures.

"Where's our scout?" the tiger demon growled. "That rabbit who claimed to know this forest like the back of its paw, the one who promised to lead us out of this mess—where is it now?"
