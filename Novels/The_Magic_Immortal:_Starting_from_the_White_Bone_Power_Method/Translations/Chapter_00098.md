# Chapter 98: Hundred-Layered Mountain Seal

After the time it takes an incense stick to burn, Mo Jingchun withdrew her jade-like finger. The green light enveloping Wei Jing also converged into her fingertip before finally gathering between her brows and vanishing from sight.

She rose slowly, lowering her head as if gathering her thoughts before speaking to Wei Yulan beside her: "The Soul-Devouring Ghost Bat originally used its yin energy to unleash soul-based divine abilities..."
