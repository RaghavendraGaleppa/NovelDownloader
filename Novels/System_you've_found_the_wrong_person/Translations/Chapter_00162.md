# Chapter 161: Future Dou’s Script  

When asked how he could tell, Dou Changsheng nearly shed bitter tears.  

With his luck, how could he possibly stumble upon a stroke of fortune?  

No—he *could* obtain opportunities, but only after narrowly escaping death. They would never simply fall into his lap out of nowhere. The way things were unfolding now was entirely the work of... *Future Dou.*
