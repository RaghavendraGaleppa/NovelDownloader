# Chapter 97: Soul-Devouring Ghost Bat

The next day.

At the hour of Yin.

A cold wind carried a fine drizzle.

The inner perimeter of the Wei family's territory was already within sight.

Towering green mountains stood solemnly, embraced by winding emerald waters. The dozen-mile-long second-grade spirit vein coiled like a serpent through the landscape. On this rainy day, it formed a mist of spiritual energy in the midair, draping the mountains and rivers in a veil of ethereal beauty.
