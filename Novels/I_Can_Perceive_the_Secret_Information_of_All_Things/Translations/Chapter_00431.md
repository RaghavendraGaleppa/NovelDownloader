# Chapter 431: Demon Palace

The journey had been anything but peaceful. After slaying over three thousand demons, the five companions finally laid eyes upon the heart of the demon realm—the Demon Palace.

Identical expressions of awe crossed their faces as the same thought struck them simultaneously—none had ever witnessed such a colossal structure before. The Demon Palace rivaled an entire kingdom in size, with towering walls that pierced the clouds, countless spires stretching skyward, and demons roaming everywhere in sight.
