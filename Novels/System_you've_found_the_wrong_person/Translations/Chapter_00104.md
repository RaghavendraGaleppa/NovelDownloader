# Chapter 104: Fortune Strikes, The Twelve Celestial Signs!

The Ghost Market.

The emphasis lies on the word "ghost."

Goods here come from every corner of the land, from the farthest reaches of the world.

Divine treasures, mystical weapons, arcane techniques, immortal artifacts, hidden paradises—even people—everything imaginable can be found here.

Yet that single word "ghost" reveals the truth of it all—can any of it be real?
