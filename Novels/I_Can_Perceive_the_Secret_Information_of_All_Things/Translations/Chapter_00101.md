# Chapter 101: The Mysterious Iron Tortoise Shell – Indeed Quite Hard

Thousand-Foot Pool.

A cascading waterfall plunged into the waters, sending countless droplets flying into the air.

From the depths of the pool emerged an ancient tortoise the size of an elephant. It slowly crawled onto the shore and sprawled on the ground to bask in the sunlight, stretching its neck proudly toward the sky in a posture that might make onlookers feel somewhat inadequate.

In the ancient forest nearby, half-demonic creatures whispered among themselves.

"Turning a public space into its personal bathing pool—this is unbearable."

"We've put up with it all these years. Why not just let it be? At worst, we can always move away."
