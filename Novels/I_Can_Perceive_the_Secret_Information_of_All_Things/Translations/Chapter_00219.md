# Chapter 219: Tang Renshuai of the Demon-Suppression Division  

Such petty villains who had done wrong were easy to deal with—just a bit of intimidation was enough to make them spill the truth.  

The owner of the cooked food shop was so terrified in the interrogation room that he collapsed in fear. Dragged into the questioning chamber with Xiao Ji and Qiu Haitang present, he tearfully confessed to what they had done.  

There were three kidnappers in total, all members of Qin Liang’s clan, bearing the surname Qin.  

Though Qin Liang himself only held a fifth-rank position in the imperial court—not particularly high—his connections ran deep.
