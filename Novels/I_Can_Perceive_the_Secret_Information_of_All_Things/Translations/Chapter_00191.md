# Chapter 191: Newcomers at Yuanshen Temple, Dragon Soar County's Feng Shui Broken  

Yuanshen Temple.  

Xiao Ji returned from his outing and stepped into the main hall. He saw his master coughing while studying a chess manual, while Yingxiao was sweeping the floor, stirring up clouds of dust.  

The main hall had indeed needed a thorough cleaning for years, with thick layers of dust settled in many places.  

"Master, Yingxiao, let me introduce you," Xiao Ji turned back to fetch someone, then led Wang Yaoda inside. "This old man is the abbot of Yuanshen Temple and my master. And this woman ran away from home and is staying here temporarily..."
