# Chapter 91: The Impregnable Fourth Pass

The Fourth Pass.

General Gao and Deputy General Qian stood respectfully outside the pass, watching as a burly man approached from the distance.

His hair was as black as a crow's feathers, neatly combed back without a single strand out of place. The silver threads woven into his robes shimmered softly under the sunlight, adding depth to his imposing figure.
