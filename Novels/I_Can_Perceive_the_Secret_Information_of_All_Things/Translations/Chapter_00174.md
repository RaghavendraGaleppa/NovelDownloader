# Chapter 174: We've Got Food Again, Brothers  

"Nails" turned into the slums and arrived at a dilapidated abandoned house where seven or eight homeless children were gathered—none older than ten years old.  

The building had no intact rooms. The largest space had numerous broken roof tiles, allowing snow to drift inside.  

The children shivered from the cold, but they had managed to scavenge enough scraps—some spread on the ground to lie on, others draped over themselves as makeshift blankets. It was barely enough to keep them from freezing.
