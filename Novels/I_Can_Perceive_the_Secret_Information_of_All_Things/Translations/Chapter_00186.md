# Chapter 186: Seizing the Lamp Base in No Man's Land, The Nascent Soul Dragon Humbles Itself Among Ruins  

The crescent moon slash shattered the phantom arhat, pressing relentlessly toward Wu Xin.  

The monk's face twisted in exaggerated shock, his eyes bulging wide as he threw a punch—somehow dispersing the sword intent. Slowly, he unclenched his fist, revealing the base of the Glazed Immortal Lamp floating in his palm. Without this fragment, how could he have withstood such a razor-sharp sword intent?  

Seeing his opponent finally produce the fragment, Xiao Ji rejoiced inwardly. He raised a lamp in his hand—one missing its base—its spiritual flame flickering as it began rapidly absorbing the surrounding light.
