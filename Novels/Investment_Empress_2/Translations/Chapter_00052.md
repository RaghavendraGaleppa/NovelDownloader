# Chapter 52: Mu Rongxiao: "This... This Isn't Right, Is It?"

The purple-robed elder glanced at Li Mo, who cupped his fists and bowed slightly. "This junior greets the Mu Rong Clan Head."

Mu Ronghai stroked his beard with a smile. "Please, take a seat."

"So, this must be the Divine Weapon, Chixiao."

"The future of the Qingyuan Sect will likely see the rise of two more Sword Immortals."

The purple-robed elder smiled, not putting on the airs of a府主 (Palace Lord).

The Great Yu Dynasty was indeed the ruler of the world. However, aside from the Central Divine Continent, the other regions of the Nine Heavens and Ten Lands were governed by sects. It was somewhat like the enfeoffment system of Li Mo's past life. Great Yu was like the Zhou Dynasty, while the sects were the various lords of different sizes, ruling over their own territories.

Li Mo had read history books. The previous dynasty was not like this; it was a true case of "all land under heaven is the King's land." Later, the Martial Emperor of the Yu Dynasty raised an army, promising to govern the world together with the sects after success, which garnered countless responses and allowed him to change the dynasty. Nevertheless, officially, the other party was still a Palace Lord, on equal footing with the Qingyuan Sect Master.

Li Mo did not lose his manners.

After some small talk, it was already afternoon. The manor held a banquet to entertain them. On the table, there were all kinds of dishes: things that flew in the sky, walked on the ground, and swam in the water. Each guest had a beautiful servant girl attending to them.

"Xiao'er, in the Qingyuan Sect, without me watching over you, you haven't been slacking off, have you?"

Mu Ronghai pulled his grandson's hand over and silently sensed his condition. He suddenly let out a sound of surprise. "This doesn't seem to be the body-refining method of the Divine Weapon Peak lineage."

"You noticed, Grandfather."

Mu Rongxiao gave an embarrassed smile. "Brother Li gave this to me, but I haven't even started practicing it yet."

The grandfather and grandson's gazes fell upon him. The elder was somewhat hesitant, while the latter looked over with an inquisitive, somewhat sheepish expression.

Li Mo smiled, not minding at all. "It's fine to show it to your grandfather. It's not a book borrowed from the library, so there's no worry about it leaking."

Only then did Mu Rongxiao放心 (reassuredly) take out the manual.

"Golden Bell Shield..."

Mu Ronghai squinted his old eyes, and the light in them grew brighter the more he read. He was also a martial artist at the Observation of the Divine Realm and could tell at a glance that this was a body-refining method with a Buddhist inclination. It could not only refine the body but also eliminate distracting thoughts and cleanse the mind. It was simply the most suitable practice for Xiao'er. In fact, he had been looking for a similar martial art for some time but had found nothing. This Golden Bell Shield was likely extremely precious even among high-level martial arts.

"Li, young friend, this method is precious, but the art cannot be taught lightly..."

"I get along very well with Brother Mu Rong, and this Golden Bell Shield is perfectly suitable for him. It can be considered as finding a successor for this martial art; otherwise, it might be lost forever."

Li Mo saw through Mu Ronghai's hesitation. Whether large or small sects, they all value their own martial arts extremely highly and strictly prohibit teaching them to outsiders.
