# Chapter 196: The Finale

Xiao Ji stepped into the peasant household. The family remained unaware of the events unfolding in the town, peacefully asleep in their beds.

Suddenly, a Daoist priest materialized in their bedroom.

The farmer sensed something amiss and opened his eyes, but terror stole his voice before any sound could escape.

"Fear not, good folk. I've come to apprehend an evil presence in your home," Xiao Ji spoke, his form appearing hazy in the dim room, his words only heightening the eerie atmosphere.

The lamp on the table flickered to life of its own accord.

Without hesitation, Xiao Ji reached down and pulled back the quilt...
