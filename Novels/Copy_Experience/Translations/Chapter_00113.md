**Chapter 112: Without Integrity, One Cannot Stand Firm; Just Call Him Han...**

Han Qingyun hesitated for a long time.

Finally, after the graceful woman secretly pinched him from behind, he still mustered his courage, strode forward, knelt before Lu Xu, respectfully kowtowed several times with loud thuds, and declared loudly:

"Father often spoke of Senior's great name and always instructed us brothers to learn from Senior. From a young age, this junior has been filled with reverence for Senior. I only regret that this junior lacks the aptitude to embark on the path of cultivation, otherwise, I would surely for Senior..."
