# Chapter 445: Set Up by the Capitalists

Xu Yunfan cupped his fists in salute while simultaneously sweeping his hand over his waist to hang the True Martial Sword—which had been refined and was lingering in his Qi Sea—at his belt.

"Hahaha!"

The black-robed cultivator suddenly burst into a wild laugh, the sound so powerful it shook the pine needles from the surrounding trees, causing them to fall in a rustling shower.

Xu Yunfan's eyes twitched slightly at the sight. People like this, who would suddenly burst into laughter for no reason, were truly awkward to deal with.

"I am Li Baichuan," the man declared. "I heard that you have established a Divine Martial Foundation and cultivate the True Martial Subduing Demon Scripture?"

Xu Yunfan raised an eyebrow. "So it's a senior brother from the Blood Curse Peak. To what do I owe the pleasure?"

Li Baichuan, a Foundation Establishment cultivator from the Blood Curse Peak, practiced the path of Blood Curse Refinement. Disciples of the Blood Curse Peak cultivated the *Nine Hells Devouring Life Blood Curse Soul Gathering True Scripture*, refining the world's malevolent energy into their bodies and gathering living souls to strengthen their spiritual sense.

Li Baichuan's laughter abruptly cut off, and a fierce light flashed in his eyes. "I'm here to borrow the Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi from your body!"

Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi?

It seemed this old fellow also knew about the cultivation method of the True Martial Subduing Demon Scripture.

Xu Yunfan's expression remained unchanged. His gaze, sharp as lightning, met Li Baichuan's directly as he spoke in a calm, cold voice: "The Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi is the gathering of the world's righteous energy, following a grand and righteous path. What you cultivate, however, is the path of blood curses and living souls, using evil arts to refine souls and steal people's vital blood. These two forces, one righteous and one evil, are like fire and water; they cannot coexist. If you try to forcibly refine it, I fear that before the malevolent energy is dissolved, you will first have your five organs and six viscera burned away by this Demon-Subduing True Fire, ending in the destruction of both body and soul."

Li Baichuan let out a cold laugh. "Do you take me for a three-year-old child who knows nothing of this matter? Be sensible and hand over the Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi. Considering we are fellow sect members, I will naturally spare your life."

Hearing this, Xu Yunfan couldn't help but flash a hint of mockery.

The Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi was a crucial foundation for him to condense his Foundation using the energy of the Heavenly Gang and Earthly Sha. If it were to be extracted, even if his foundation didn't collapse, it would suffer irreparable damage. His path to cultivation would be cut short.

Xu Yunfan then reacted, a look of sudden understanding dawning on his face. "So, you want to use the Demon-Subduing Qi to cleanse the blood curse in your body?"

A faint smile appeared on his face as he said leisurely, "It seems your path of cultivation ends here, Senior Brother. If I were you, I would choose to be reborn and start over. Why go to all this trouble and effort to seek my Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi?"

Li Baichuan snorted coldly; he had naturally anticipated Xu Yunfan's refusal.

"Enough nonsense. Will you hand it over or not?"

"What if I do? What if I don't?"

As Xu Yunfan spoke, his right hand had already silently rested on the hilt of the True Martial Sword at his waist. His knuckles exerted a slight force, and spiritual power burst from his fingertips, causing the True Martial Sword to slowly awaken.

A faint shadow of a Black Tortoise appeared around him, the intertwined forms of the turtle and snake radiating a dignified and majestic aura.

The Northern Polar Demon-Subduing Qi flowed through his meridians, forming a stark contrast with the grayish-white malevolent energy swirling around Li Baichuan. It was like the sun against ice, inherently mutually exclusive.

What truly stirred suspicion in his heart was that although he had established his Divine Martial Foundation under the gaze of the three True Lords, very few people actually knew about it. True Lords have their own dignity and wouldn't go around boasting about it.

Where had this Blood Curse Peak cultivator come from to know about this?

In a flash of insight, Xu Yunfan's heart stirred, and he had already figured out the key to the matter. Someone must have used divination to pry into the heavenly secrets, trying to find out his background. However, because he cultivated the "Turtle Breath Soul-Hiding Technique" from the True Martial Subduing Demon Scripture, as well as the Azure Dragon Thunder Pearl Locking Calamity and Suppressing Fortune Method bestowed by the Qingyuan True Lord, their attempts to stir up karma had failed.

Thus, they sent this brute to make a direct probe.

Xu Yunfan had a strong suspicion of which True Lord was behind this masterpiece.

Damn it, I'm getting caught in the crossfire!

Xu Yunfan sighed inwardly, feeling a bit annoyed at the same time.

*Just wait until I get the chance; I'll definitely settle the score with you.*

However, through this indirect probing, Xu Yunfan also understood that the other party was being used as a pawn, so he wasn't too worried that taking action would lead to any serious consequences.

Seeing Xu Yunfan's silence, Li Baichuan mistook it for fear and smugly licked his lips. "Rest assured, I only need to extract seventy percent of your vital energy. It won't cause your foundation to collapse. You can just find another source later to make up for it..."

As he spoke, he pulled out a blood-red jade bottle from his chest. "This is the 'Soul-Devouring Bottle,' specifically designed to deal with people like you..."

"Senior Brother Li."

Xu Yunfan suddenly interrupted, his voice as cold as ice. "Do you know the 307th rule of the Primordial Sect's code?"
