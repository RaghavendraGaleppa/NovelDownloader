# Chapter 456: The Swordmaster of the Desolate Heaven Kingdom  

Po Wianxian opened his eyes, standing frozen in place—he had slain his beloved Hongyi.  

Yun Tiankai turned back to see Hongyi shielding him from the fatal strike, guilt surging through his heart as he glared at Po Wianxian with fury.  

The Scattered Cloud Blade danced in Yun Tiankai’s grip as the two soared away from the Cloud-Plucking Tower, their figures flickering through the air. By the time Po Wianxian regained some clarity, the venom of the clouds had already seeped deep into his bones.
