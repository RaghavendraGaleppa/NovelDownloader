# Chapter 184: The Radical Faction of Legalism  

The path to self-destruction—  

Dou Changsheng was already charging ahead at full speed.  

He had come to realize one thing: being cautious and reserved wouldn’t necessarily prevent others from testing him, while acting recklessly and arrogantly might not reduce the challenges either. But at the very least, he’d die more comfortably.  

It was a simple truth—even before execution, one deserved a full meal.
