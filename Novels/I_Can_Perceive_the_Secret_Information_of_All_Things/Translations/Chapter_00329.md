# Chapter 329: Last Time You Said You Were Visiting Your Parents, Where Exactly Did You Go?

As its name suggested, Lion Grove was filled with lions.  

The area earned its name because a central grassland was completely surrounded and shaded by dense woods. This place teemed with lion demons, and their ruler was one of these ferocious creatures.  

This was the domain of lions. Anyone who dared utter the word "tiger" would immediately find themselves pounced upon by over a dozen lions.
