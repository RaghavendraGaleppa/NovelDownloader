# Chapter 134: Ancient Medicinal Field

Unlike the ruins Lu Xu had previously encountered, this site was not completely dilapidated. Its overall preservation was excellent, with no damage to the buildings or structures.

As Lu Xu, who had just entered the area, cast his gaze around, he felt that this must have been a secluded dwelling for fellow cultivators, as the various architectural facilities were remarkably complete.

A rich spiritual aura assailed him. There was indeed a second-tier spirit vein here. Based on the spiritual energy's density, Lu Xu surmised it should be a second-tier low-grade...
