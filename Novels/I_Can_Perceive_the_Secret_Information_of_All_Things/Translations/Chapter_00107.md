# Chapter 107: The Great Demon Lord Has Come to Cloudpeak City?

Xiao Ji could tell this was a swordsman who valued face but lacked courage.

After exchanging pleasantries, the two introduced themselves and made small talk for a while. Only then did Three Seasons Blade relax enough to pick up the two mantis claws from the ground and store them in his own spatial pouch.

"May I ask why you've come to this wilderness, fellow Daoist?" Xiao Ji inquired, already knowing the answer.

"I heard rumors that monsters often appear along this desolate path, so I came to uphold justice," Three Seasons Blade replied.
