# Chapter 467: Defying Fate

Xiao Ji successfully merged the Nine Treasures, only to witness them fuse with the last wisp of the First Sage's lingering shadow. To his astonishment, the sage's flesh and soul contained within the treasures coalesced once more into physical form.  

Though the reconstituted being retained but a fraction of its former power and appeared remarkably feeble, Xiao Ji remained indifferent to the Nine Treasures' ultimate transformation. His sole concern lay in the completion of the fusion itself.
