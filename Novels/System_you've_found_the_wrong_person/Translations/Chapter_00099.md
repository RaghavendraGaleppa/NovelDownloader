# Chapter 99: A New Race, The Birth of the Corpse Ghost (Makeup for Yesterday)

The underground palace trembled incessantly.

Towering stone pillars swayed precariously, as if on the verge of collapse. 

A wrinkled, aged hand pressed against one of the pillars. The elderly Sword Maiden steadied herself against the shaking stone, her clouded eyes widening with astonishment as she surveyed the violently quaking underground chamber.

Nearby, the younger Sword Maiden gathered her skirts in one hand, her expression mirroring the older woman's shock as the ancient structure continued its ominous tremors.
