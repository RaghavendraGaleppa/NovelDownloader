# Chapter 109: Old Acquaintances in the Market City

Two blinding rays of light blasted towards the earthy yellow grand formation. Within the formation, hundreds of black-robed bandit cultivators raised their heads, a deep look of despair appearing in their eyes as they gazed at the two god-like figures in the sky.

“Rumble!”

Not long after, accompanied by a resounding boom, the grand formation was breached. Two streaks of light immediately surged into the market city, sweeping all before them like wolves entering a flock of sheep.

Divine light flashed through, blood splattered, and cries, curses, and pleas echoed endlessly.
