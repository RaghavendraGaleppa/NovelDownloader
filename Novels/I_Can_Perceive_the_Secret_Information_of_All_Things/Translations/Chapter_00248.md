# Chapter 248: The Taoist Annihilates Thirteen, The Western Monk's Summoning Art  

The afternoon sun hung low in the western sky, casting a golden glow over the tranquil sea, blending the hues of water and heavens into a breathtaking vista.  

Xiao Ji's thoughts stirred as he released a small boat onto the water. Steering it toward the serene depths of the ocean, he stood upright with hands clasped behind his back. The vessel glided swiftly across the waves, cutting through the surface with the speed of a modern speedboat.
