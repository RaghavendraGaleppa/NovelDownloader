Chapter 1: The Desolate Ancient Continent

On the Desolate Ancient Continent, powerhouses reigned supreme, and the strong preyed upon the weak. Martial Dao was the only path to survival.

In the Azure Dragon City, the Yan Clan's training grounds.

"Yan Xuan, your meridians are damaged, your Dantian is shattered. You are a cripple who can no longer practice martial arts. Hand over the position of Young Master to your cousin Yan Feng."

"Yan Xuan, you have been a genius for too long. It's time to step down."

Amidst the clamor of voices, a young man in white sat cross-legged on the training platform. His handsome face was pale, but his eyes were as deep as the abyss.

He was Yan Xuan, the former genius of the Yan Clan.

Three years ago, he was the number one genius in Azure Dragon City, sweeping across all peers. But in a battle for the Heaven Mystery Pill, he was betrayed by his most trusted brother and had his meridians severed and Dantian shattered, turning from a genius into a cripple.

For the past three years, he had endured humiliation and insults, clinging to the hope of recovering.

"Everyone, please be quiet."

At this moment, a clear and cold voice sounded.

A girl in a light green dress walked in. She had a delicate and beautiful face, but her eyes were filled with indifference.

She was Su Ling, Yan Xuan's fiancée.

"Su Ling is here! The Su Clan has come to dissolve the engagement!"

"Yan Xuan is finished. His fiancée has come to break off the engagement. This is adding insult to injury."

The crowd whispered.

Su Ling walked up to Yan Xuan, her eyes sweeping over him without a trace of emotion. She took out a marriage contract and said coldly: "Yan Xuan, our engagement was based on your talent. Now that you are a cripple, this engagement is no longer valid. Today, I am here to break off the engagement."

The words were heartless and cruel.

Yan Xuan raised his head, looking at the girl he once loved deeply. He asked calmly: "Su Ling, do you really want to do this?"

"Of course." Su Ling's tone was firm. "I, Su Ling, will not marry a废人废物."

"Good." Yan Xuan nodded, a hint of a bitter smile on his lips. "Since you have made up your mind, I won't keep you. But remember, what you gave up today, you will regret for the rest of your life."

"Regret?" Su Ling sneered. "A废人废物 like you, what qualifications do you have to make me regret? Yan Xuan, don't be arrogant. From today on, our paths are different. I hope you don't come to me in the future."

After speaking, she turned and left, not looking back.

The crowd dispersed, leaving Yan Xuan alone sitting on the training platform, his figure desolate.

"Su Ling... Yan Feng..."

Yan Xuan clenched his fists, his nails digging into his flesh, blood dripping.

He did not despair. Instead, a fire of anger burned in his heart.

"Since the heavens have given me a second chance, I will not let it go to waste!"

Just now, when Su Ling broke off the engagement, a stream of information poured into Yan Xuan's mind.

He learned that he had traveled from another world and possessed the memories of a peerless powerhouse from the Primordial Era.

In the Primordial Era, there was a supreme being known as the Desolate Ancient Emperor. He cultivated the Desolate Ancient Scripture, which contained the secrets of the universe and the laws of the Great Dao.

After the Desolate Ancient Emperor fell, his soul fragment traveled through time and space and merged with Yan Xuan.

Now, Yan Xuan possesses the memories of the Desolate Ancient Emperor and the Desolate Ancient Scripture.

"Desolate Ancient Scripture, Desolate Ancient Body..."

Yan Xuan's eyes became brighter and brighter.

The Desolate Ancient Scripture is the supreme cultivation technique of the Primordial Era. It can refine the body and spirit, and ultimately achieve the Desolate Ancient Body, a body that is indestructible and eternal.

With the Desolate Ancient Scripture, he can not only repair his shattered Dantian and severed meridians, but also become even stronger than before!

"Yan Feng, Su Ling, you think I'm a废人废物? Just wait and see!"

Yan Xuan stood up, his eyes shining with a sharp light.

At this moment, a servant ran over in a panic: "Young Master, not good! Young Master Yan Feng has led people to the door, saying that he wants to take over the Young Master's residence!"

"What?" Yan Xuan's eyes turned cold.

The Young Master's residence is the symbol of the Yan Clan's Young Master. If Yan Feng took over, it would be a complete denial of Yan Xuan's status.

"He's really impatient." Yan Xuan sneered.

He had just obtained the Desolate Ancient Scripture, and someone was送上门来 to be a test subject.

"Let's go, let's go see what my 'good cousin' wants to do."

Yan Xuan walked towards the Young Master's residence with large strides.

At this moment, in front of the Young Master's residence, a group of people were making a scene.

Leading was a young man in brock clothes, with a arrogant expression. He was Yan Feng, the cousin Yan Xuan mentioned.

Behind him followed a group of guards, and among them, there was a middle-aged man with a calm expression, his eyes sharp.

This middle-aged man is the steward of the Yan Clan, Yan Lie, who is also Yan Feng's backer.

"Yan Xuan, get out here!"

Yan Feng shouted loudly, his voice spreading throughout the Young Master's residence.

"Yan Feng, what do you want?"

Yan Xuan walked out, his expression calm.

"Yan Xuan, you are a废人废物, not qualified to live in the Young Master's residence. Today, I am here to take over." Yan Feng said with a smirk.

"Take over? With what qualification?" Yan Xuan asked coldly.

"Qualification? Hahaha!" Yan Feng laughed wildly. "In this world, the strong are king. I am now at the eighth level of Qi Refining, and you are a废人废物. This is the qualification!"

As he spoke, Yan Feng suddenly attacked, his palm carrying a powerful force, slapping towards Yan Xuan's chest.

This palm was swift and fierce, clearly intending to seriously injure Yan Xuan.

"Be careful, Young Master!" The guards shouted.

Yan Xuan stood still, watching the palm approach.

Just as the palm was about to hit him, Yan Xuan moved.

He didn't dodge, but instead took a step forward, his right hand forming a claw, grabbing towards Yan Xuan's wrist.

This move was extremely fast, like lightning, and it was the Desolate Ancient Scripture's technique - Desolate Ancient Claw!

"Desolate Ancient Claw? Impossible!" Yan Lie, the steward, was shocked.

He never expected that Yan Xuan, a废人废物, could use such a profound martial technique.

But it was too late to stop.

Yan Xuan's claw had already grasped Yan Feng's wrist.

"Crack!"

With a crisp sound, Yan Feng's wrist was directly broken by Yan Xuan.

"Ah!" Yan Feng screamed in pain, his face instantly turned pale.

"You... you recovered?" Yan Feng looked at Yan Xuan in horror.

"Not only recovered, but also stronger than before." Yan Xuan said coldly, and then kicked out.

Yan Feng was kicked flying, crashing heavily into a wall, spitting out a mouthful of blood.

The scene was silent.

Everyone looked at Yan Xuan in shock, their eyes full of disbelief.

This废人废物, how could he suddenly become so powerful?

"Yan Lie, it's your turn." Yan Xuan turned his gaze to the steward Yan Lie.

"Yan Xuan, you dare to attack the Young Master? This is a serious crime!" Yan Lie shouted, trying to suppress Yan Xuan with his status.

"Serious crime? Hahaha!" Yan Xuan laughed coldly. "Yan Feng attacked first, and I defended myself. If you want to talk about crimes, then Yan Feng's crime of attacking the Young Master is even greater!"

"You..." Yan Lie was speechless.

"Yan Lie, don't use your status to intimidate me. Today, I want to let everyone see what kind of person Yan Feng is." Yan Xuan said loudly.

He turned to the crowd and said: "Three years ago, I was betrayed by my most trusted brother and had my meridians severed and Dantian shattered. That brother was Yan Feng!"

"What?" The crowd was in an uproar.

Three years ago, Yan Xuan was attacked and his meridians were severed. Everyone thought it was an accident, but they never expected that it was Yan Feng's doing.

"Yan Feng, you have the nerve to say that I am a废人废物? You are the one who made me a废人废物!" Yan Xuan roared.

Yan Feng's face was ashen, and he couldn't say a word.

"Yan Lie, you are Yan Feng's backer. You must have known the truth long ago, but you kept it from everyone. You are an accomplice!" Yan Xuan pointed at Yan Lie and said.

Yan Lie's face changed dramatically. He never expected that Yan Xuan would know the truth.

"Since you all want to kill me, then don't blame me for being ruthless." Yan Xuan's eyes were cold.

He stepped forward, his momentum rising.

"Today, I will settle the old and new scores with you all!"

Yan Xuan's voice was like thunder, echoing over the Yan Clan's training grounds.

At this moment, everyone felt that the former genius had returned.

And this time, he will be more terrifying than ever before!

The Desolate Ancient Emperor's legacy, the Desolate Ancient Scripture, will help him step onto the peak of the Martial Dao!

The story has just begun...
