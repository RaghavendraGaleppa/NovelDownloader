# Chapter 94: Sometimes Solving a Case Just Requires a Mouth

Xiao Ji blew up the pavilion with a tremendous explosion.

The loud noise quickly drew a crowd—disciples of Qicao Mountain came running, along with the old steward. Duan An and Zhong Shenzhi also hurried over to see what the commotion was about.  

The explosion had been deafening.  

It was impossible not to be curious.  

Everyone stared at the now-clothesless Taoist.  

"Taoist Xiao," the old steward stepped forward, "this pond is off-limits for swimming."  

I wasn’t trying to swim… Xiao Ji thought.
