# Chapter 157: Bizarre Scenes Unfolding One After Another

When Wang Chan returned to Yunbai Alley from the Duty Hall, Xiushan Street under the setting sun and the distant scenery of the suburban mountains and waters were picturesque, too beautiful to take in all at once.

Yet Wang Chan had no mind for the beautiful scenery. Upon returning to his courtyard, he sent Black Tiger to catch insects in the pond and had Black Water practice earth escape in the underground space to play with mud. As for himself, he lay down on the recliner in the courtyard and closed his eyes.
