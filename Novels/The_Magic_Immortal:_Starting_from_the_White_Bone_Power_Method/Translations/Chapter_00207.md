# Chapter 207: Forbidden Talisman

The howls of demonic beasts and spectral entities sounded the same.  

Before crossing Tianfeng Mountain, their cries had been unsettling and grating to the ears.  

But now, within the boundaries of Beifeng Mountain, the rising and falling chorus inexplicably carried a strange, almost pleasant rhythm.  

Wang Chan faced the biting night wind, his heart swelling with such exhilaration that he nearly wished to engage in a mountain song duel with those beasts and spirits.
