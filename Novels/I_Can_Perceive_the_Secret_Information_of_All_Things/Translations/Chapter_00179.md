# Chapter 179: The Sinister Monk

As Xiao Ji left the small Buddhist hall, the flames in the lamps suddenly turned a murky yellow. The withered, dead monk within slowly opened his eyes and gazed in the direction of Xiao Ji's departure, sighing, "Amitabha, may all be well."

...

Xiao Ji had left the small Buddhist hall. Though he had seen the fragment of the immortal lamp he sought, he had also sensed danger and thus refrained from acting rashly. At the time, he hadn't thought much of it, but after leaving the hall, a realization suddenly dawned upon him.
