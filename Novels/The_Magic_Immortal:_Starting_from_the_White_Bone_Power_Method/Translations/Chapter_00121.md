# Chapter 121: Inviting Fellow Daoist Wang to Step Forward

The distant black giant tortoise, towering like a mountain, took the four people hiding in the cloud canopy—Wang Chan and his companions—by surprise.

Ning Jingming's face darkened—though to be fair, his already dark and ugly complexion couldn't get much darker. Still, his brows furrowed deeply, his expression grave.

Li Ping wore a constipated look, absentmindedly stroking his chin.
