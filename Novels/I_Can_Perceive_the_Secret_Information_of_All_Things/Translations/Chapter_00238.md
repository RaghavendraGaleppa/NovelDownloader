# Chapter 238: Luring the Fish

The melancholic notes of a four-stringed pipa began to play, their mysterious melody swirling above the sunken coffin lake with no discernible source.

A biting chill swept through the air as if winter had descended abruptly.

All the cultivators present grew alert, their eyes scanning the surroundings in tense silence.

Such phenomena usually heralded the arrival of some formidable figure - background music swelling, special effects at their peak, followed by a dashing character materializing dramatically from the icy winds.

Yet disappointingly, nothing of the sort occurred. Only the relentless howl of the freezing wind persisted.
