# Chapter 138: Late Foundation Establishment

A long, black banner flapped in the wind, and a surging, boundless demonic mist billowed forth, obscuring the sky and sun, instantly transforming the area into a demonic domain.

The Eight-Armed Fiend was summoned. It let out an excited roar, and when it saw the two corpses, a faint red glow appeared in its eyes, its greed undisguised.

"Devour these two souls. If you still can't break through to the Late Foundation Establishment stage after this, then there will be no need for you to exist!" Lu Xu's calm voice echoed in its ears.
