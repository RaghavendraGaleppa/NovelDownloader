# Chapter 175: Misfortune Strikes Reality

Wang Tong was a great Confucian scholar of his generation. His disciples and students were spread across various states, and even excluding those who merely attended his lectures, the number of true inner disciples was considerable. Among so many, however, only a handful could truly be considered Wang Tong's favorites.

These favored disciples each had their own lives to lead and could not remain by his side indefinitely.
