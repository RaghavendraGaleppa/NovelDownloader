# Chapter 249: Whale Fall Sea, Ambergris

Dios didn't consider killing two Foundation Establishment cultivators particularly remarkable. She had slain far more people before, even annihilating an entire territory that refused to worship the Heavenly God. In her view, those who worshipped Satan were devils that needed to be purged swiftly.

The summoned angel hurled the two island-nation cultivators off the cliff before vanishing back into the black mist.
