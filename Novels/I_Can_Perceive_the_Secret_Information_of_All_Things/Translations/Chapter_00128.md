# Chapter 128: The Glazed Immortal Lamp

Within the Dao City, the paid reading service drew complaints from many cultivators. The duration of a single incense stick's burning was far too short to grasp the essence of any cultivation manual. Thus, most practitioners who entered would purchase several days' worth of time—an expenditure that demanded a considerable amount of spirit stones.

Though discounted services existed, the overall cost still weighed heavily on one's purse. Yet for many rogue cultivators and self-taught seekers of the Dao—those without access to sect techniques but still yearning for knowledge—this remained their only option.
