Chapter 73: The Death of the Black Wolf

"Fall!" Following a loud shout, golden blades rained down from the sky as the second wave of attacks began.

The Black Wolf roared and bellowed. No matter how much fur it shed, it could no longer unleash an attack like the previous one. Its entire coat of fur had been cultivated on its body for hundreds of years, tempered by the essence of the sun and moon, forming its most terrifying weapon. As a result, it was completely depleted in just one round under the formation's assault. Now, facing the second wave of golden blades...
