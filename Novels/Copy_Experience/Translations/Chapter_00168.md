Chapter 166: Behind the Bronze Gate

Lu Xu wished he could pull out the token and press it against the bronze gate right now, opening the restriction and entering the Core Formation spiritual land. His heart burned with an eager anticipation, ready to stir.

But ultimately, he wasn't so easily manipulated by greed. After giving the great gate a few intense glances, he turned around and continued to observe the situation in the passage. Now was not the time to attempt it. For him, using the token wasn't the issue; the problem was how to fend off the attacks from those several Golden Core cultivators after he used it.
