# Chapter 245: Journey to the East Sea - Western Cultivators and Island Cultivators  

The East Sea stretched endlessly before them, vast and boundless.  

A massive sea vessel cut through the inky waves, its hull rising and falling with the restless ocean. From a distance, it resembled nothing more than a flickering lantern adrift in an eternal night.  

The dark waters crashed against the ship's sides with heavy, thunderous blows, each impact resonating with the sea's unspoken fury.
