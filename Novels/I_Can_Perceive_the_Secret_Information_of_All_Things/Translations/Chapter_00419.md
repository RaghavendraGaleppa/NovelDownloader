# Chapter 419: The Great Wall of Human Destiny  

Yuan Shen Temple, front courtyard.  

The middle-aged and elderly residents of Willow Town still flocked to this courtyard daily, treating it as an excellent place for exercise. It seemed they had forgotten that this was once a sacred site of worship, a place for making wishes.  

People knew of those who frequented this place, yet the moment they stepped beyond the temple gates, their memories would fade...
