# Chapter 144: Substitute Calamity Talisman

Three days later.

As the first rays of morning sunlight bathed the streets, Wang Chan stepped down from his beast-drawn carriage and strode leisurely toward the Breeze and Drizzle Pavilion. 

Two late-stage Qi Refining guards at the entrance moved forward to inspect him. Noticing their frowns as they examined his rogue cultivator identification token, Wang Chan produced his Talisman Academy token and handed it over instead.

"Please proceed."
