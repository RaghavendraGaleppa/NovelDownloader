# Chapter 153: A Perilous Question

After a while, Xiao Ji appeared.

He was led in by a taciturn servant and joined the other guests waiting for breakfast. By then, the elderly woman who had announced the manor's rules had already left, leaving only the murmuring guests in hushed discussions.

Several people glanced at Xiao Ji, all thinking the same thing: this Taoist priest was the only one who arrived late and hadn't heard the nine rules. He would make perfect test material for the rules.
