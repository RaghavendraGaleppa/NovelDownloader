# Chapter 132: The Marketplace

The shoebill felt utterly powerless as it was brought to this horrifying place.

Someone held a sharp knife, gutting a black pig on a bloodstained table where various crimson organs and strips of meat were displayed. Nearby, another person was slaughtering fish. Though the fish struggled desperately, it couldn't escape the butcher's cruel grasp. Witnessing its own guts being scooped out, the fish must have been terrified—its eyes remained wide open even in death.

The shoebill thought of itself—when it ate fish, it simply swallowed them whole...
