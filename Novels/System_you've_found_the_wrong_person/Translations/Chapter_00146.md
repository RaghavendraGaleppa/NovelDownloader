# Chapter 145: Wilderness Journey, Meeting an Acquaintance Again

The ruins of Seven Extremes Pass.

Dou Changsheng returned to this familiar place, gazing at the broken walls and collapsed structures. He couldn't help but pat the donkey he was riding.  

Suddenly, openings appeared on both sides of the donkey's body, revealing a steel-forged skeleton covered with erect feathers. As the massive wings began to flap, the donkey lifted off the ground and soared into the sky.
