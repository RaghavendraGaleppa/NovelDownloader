# Chapter 185: Subduing Demons Imminent, Arhats Offer Aid  

Within the abandoned ancient city, atop a stone roof, Monk Wuxin tore off the scalp he had seized and tossed it aside. Sitting cross-legged, he felt an unusual lightness in his heart. Unable to resist, he took out the immortal lamp pedestal and began to toy with it.  

Since joining Qingyin Temple, he had originally intended to devote himself wholeheartedly to Buddhism. Yet fate had shown him the immense power of the immortal lamp fragments. Over the years, he had waited patiently, and when the moment came—when his master was locked in a standoff with the soul of an evil monk—he seized the opportunity to steal the lamp pedestal.  

"Pity my strength was insufficient... I couldn't take the lamp body as well..."
