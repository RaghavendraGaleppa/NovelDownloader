# Chapter 189: The Shaoyang Sword, the Ruthlessness of Sages  

The Yin-Yang School had fled entirely.  

It wouldn’t be difficult to capture their ordinary disciples with a thorough search, but apprehending the sect’s leader would prove far more challenging.  

Truly, one had to admire the audacity of a man who could overthrow his superiors. Such shamelessness and cunning, slippery as a ghost, was a skill in itself. Unlike Wang Tong, who couldn’t let go of his pride—otherwise, he wouldn’t have met such a clean end.
