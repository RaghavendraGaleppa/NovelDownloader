# Chapter 82: A Taoist Priest Squatting in the Yard

The multicolored snake slithered soundlessly into the scholar's home. Barring any unforeseen circumstances, the scholar would soon be devoured by the monstrous serpent, and Willow Town would lose one more resident.

Perhaps someday in the future, the neighbors might remember him and ask each other if they'd seen the scholar. Most would likely conclude he had traveled far away. A kind soul might report it to the authorities, who would send a few constables to investigate. Unless signs of a struggle were found, the matter would likely be dismissed without further inquiry.
