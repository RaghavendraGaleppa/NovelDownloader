# Chapter 266: Xiao Jiqiao Enters the Town Immortal Tower, The Ailing Spear Immortal Han Sandian

The white bear demon of Zhuxian Town had vanished.

This matter greatly displeased the Nascent Soul wolf overseeing the town. Though it had long looked down upon the white bear demon who had gotten its position through connections—its cultivation barely at Golden Core level yet managing the array control device, something that should rightfully be under the wolf's control—there was nothing to be done. The bear had been bold enough to recognize a foster grandfather, leaving the wolf no choice but to endure the situation.
