# Chapter 447: That Was My Lost Youth, I Must Reclaim It

At the secluded meditation site of the True Star Temple's master, Xiao Ji waited for three consecutive days.

There were less than two days remaining until the seventh-day memorial of Zhan Duanfeng's passing. Zhan Duanfeng had always kept track of the time of his death, yet now he suddenly found himself preoccupied with other matters. This powerful Taoist, who had connections even in the underworld, seemed to have completely forgotten about this affair.
