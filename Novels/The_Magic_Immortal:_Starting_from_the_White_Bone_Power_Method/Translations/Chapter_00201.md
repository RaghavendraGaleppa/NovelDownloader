# Chapter 201: Hidden Spirit, Entwined Form!

Half a month later.

Under the cover of a moonless night with howling winds, Wang Chan restrained all traces of his magical power. Not even daring to release his spiritual sense, he relied solely on the piercing light in his eyes that turned the dark night into day as he traversed the rugged mountain ranges with pure physical strength.

Whoosh!

In a single bound, he crossed a hundred zhang, leaping over the mountain stream ahead as distant peaks loomed in the darkness.
