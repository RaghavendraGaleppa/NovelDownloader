# Chapter 369: The Terrifying Taoist Temple  

The two thieves were utterly terrified by the giant peacock. Throughout their careers as burglars, they had encountered many demons, but a peacock of this stature could only be a demon king.  

The spiritual energy flowing through the secret realm left them unable to remain calm, clouding their judgment entirely.  

So, just what kind of existence was the Yuanshen Temple?
